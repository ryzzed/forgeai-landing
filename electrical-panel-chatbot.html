Panel Upgrade Leads Have Built-In Urgency

There are two types of electrical panel leads: safety-driven and load-driven. Safety leads come from homeowners who discovered they have a Federal Pacific Stab-Lok or Zinsco panel -- known fire hazards that insurance companies often refuse to cover. Load-driven leads come from homeowners adding an EV charger, heat pump, or hot tub who just learned their 100-amp panel cannot support the new load.
