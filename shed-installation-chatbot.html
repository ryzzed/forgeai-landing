Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a shed company sell more sheds?

A chatbot qualifies intended use, recommends the right size, identifies permit requirements, and explains foundation options — all before your sales rep gets involved. It captures homeowners comparing prefab vs custom, converting browsers into booked site assessments or delivery appointments 24/7.

What should a shed installation chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask what they plan to store, available yard space, preferred style, whether they want prefab or custom on-site build, and their approximate budget. Intended use drives size recommendation — a riding mower with accessories needs a 12x20 minimum, while simple bike storage works in an 8x10.

Can the chatbot identify when a shed permit is required?

Yes. Most jurisdictions require permits for sheds over 120 square feet or within a certain setback from property lines. The chatbot asks about desired size and proximity to fences and property lines — flagging permit requirements upfront so homeowners are informed before ordering.
