What are the advantages of pavers over poured concrete?
Pavers resist cracking because individual units flex with ground movement rather than fracturing like a solid slab. Individual damaged pavers can be replaced without patching the entire surface. Pavers also offer better drainage through permeable joints and come in hundreds of styles and colors.
