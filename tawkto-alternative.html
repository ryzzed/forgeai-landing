Common questions

Can I run ChatFlow alongside Tawk.to to compare?

Yes — both are script tags and don't conflict. Add ChatFlow to a specific page or set it as the primary widget on pages where you don't have agents available, while keeping Tawk.to active elsewhere. Evaluate over a few weeks.

Does ChatFlow have any ongoing per-agent or per-conversation fees?

No. Free plan has 1,000 conversations/month. Starter ($39/mo) has 5,000. Pro ($79/mo) is unlimited. No per-conversation charges, no agent seats, no add-on fees for AI.

How does ChatFlow train its AI for my specific business?

You write a description of your business — services, hours, pricing, policies, common FAQs — in plain text in the bot configuration. The AI uses that as its knowledge base to answer visitor questions. No flow builder, no scripted paths.

What happens when a visitor asks something the AI doesn't know?

The bot politely says it doesn't have that information and suggests the visitor contact you directly — with your contact details if you've included them in the training content. It won't make up answers.
