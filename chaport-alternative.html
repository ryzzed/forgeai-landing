Common questions

When should I stick with Chaport?

Chaport is a good choice if you have staff who actively monitor and respond to chats during business hours and genuinely want live human-to-human conversations. Chaport's interface is clean and simple for that use case. ChatFlow is better when you want automation — conversations handled without any human involvement.

Does ChatFlow have a free plan like Chaport?

Yes — ChatFlow's free plan includes 1,000 AI conversations per month. This is enough for most small businesses to get started, validate that the chatbot is working, and see if it's worth upgrading to Starter ($39/month for 5,000 conversations). No credit card required.

How does ChatFlow pricing compare to Chaport?

Chaport has a free plan (1 operator, limited) and Pro at $23/operator/month. For a solo business owner that's $23/month — comparable to ChatFlow's free plan. For 2+ operators, Chaport Pro starts at $46/month+, while ChatFlow Starter is $39/month flat for unlimited dashboard users and 5,000 AI conversations.
