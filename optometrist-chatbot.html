Common questions

Can the chatbot handle urgent vision questions (eye pain, sudden vision change)?

Configure the bot with clear instructions for potential emergencies: "If you're experiencing sudden vision loss, eye pain, flashes or floaters, or eye injury, please call us immediately at [phone] or go to your nearest urgent care or emergency room." This ensures the bot doesn't cause harm by engaging in lengthy conversation when a patient needs immediate care.

Can I use the chatbot to remind patients about annual exams?

ChatFlow is a website widget — it engages website visitors rather than sending outbound messages. For annual reminder campaigns, you'd pair it with an email marketing tool. The chatbot is most valuable for capturing new patients and answering questions from existing patients who return to your website to book.

What about pediatric eye exams?

Include your youngest age for exams, what a pediatric exam involves, and any programs like InfantSEE in your system prompt. Parents researching eye care for young children have specific questions about the exam process for kids — answering these builds confidence before they call to schedule.
