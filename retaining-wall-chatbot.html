Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a retaining wall company get more jobs?

A chatbot qualifies wall height (the key variable that determines whether a permit is required), captures erosion and slope failure emergencies before the homeowner calls three competitors, and educates on material options so your estimator arrives with a pre-sold product preference.

What should a retaining wall chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask the approximate wall height and length, the slope condition (active erosion vs cosmetic grading), proximity to structures, material preference, and whether this is new construction or a failing wall replacement. Walls over 4 feet typically require permits and engineered drawings — flagging this upfront prevents pricing surprises.

Can the chatbot capture emergency calls when a retaining wall is failing?

Yes. When a homeowner reports active leaning, cracking, or soil movement, the chatbot flags it as a structural emergency — particularly if the wall is within 10 feet of the home or a driveway. It collects contact information and triggers an urgent callback, capturing high-value repair and rebuild jobs before foundation damage occurs.
