Questions veterinary clinics ask us

Will it give dangerous medical advice to pet owners?

No — you define exactly what the bot covers. Keep it focused on scheduling, services, and general information, and set the emergency protocol to always direct urgent situations to call you directly or go to the nearest 24-hour emergency vet.

Can it integrate with our booking system?

ChatFlow can explain your services and link owners to your existing booking page (VetHero, Weave, PetDesk, your website, etc.). Direct calendar integration is on our roadmap.

How do I update the bot for seasonal services or new staff?

Log into your ChatFlow dashboard, click your bot, and update the training information. Changes go live instantly — no redeployment needed.

Is pet conversation data secure?

Yes. Conversations are stored securely and only accessible to your clinic via the dashboard. No sensitive medical data should be volunteered in chat — the bot is designed for general inquiries, not medical records.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that allows a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, VetMatrix, DVM Elite, or custom HTML. Setup takes under 2 minutes and requires no developer.
