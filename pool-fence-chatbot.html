How does a chatbot help pool fence companies get more jobs?

What are the standard pool fence code requirements?
Most jurisdictions require a pool barrier of at least 48 inches height, self-latching gates that open away from the pool, no climbable horizontal rails within 45 inches of the ground, and maximum 4-inch gaps between vertical pickets. ChatFlow helps homeowners understand local requirements and creates urgency around compliance deadlines.
What pool fence materials does the chatbot help homeowners choose between?
ChatFlow explains aluminum (durable, code-compliant, low maintenance), glass (frameless luxury look, maximum visibility), mesh removable fence (affordable temporary barrier), and vinyl (privacy option for complete yard enclosure).
