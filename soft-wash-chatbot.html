Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a soft wash company get more jobs?

A chatbot qualifies surface type, stain type, and trigger event — then explains why soft wash chemical treatment kills algae roots for 1-2 year results vs pressure washing that removes surface growth temporarily. It books appointments 24/7 and captures peak-demand spring leads before competitors respond.

What should a soft wash chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask what surfaces need washing, primary stain type (black algae streaks, green mildew, oxidation, general dirt), and what triggered the inquiry (HOA notice, listing the home, annual maintenance, visible growth). Home sale preparation and HOA violation notices are the highest-urgency leads — they convert same-day.

Can the chatbot capture roof soft wash leads separately from house washing?

Yes. Roof soft washing is a premium service — typically $300-$700 for asphalt shingles and an important upsell for homeowners already booking a house wash. The chatbot asks about roof staining and explains that pressure washing a roof voids most shingle warranties, positioning soft wash as the only safe method.
