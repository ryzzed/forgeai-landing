What are the egress window well requirements for finished basements?
IRC egress requirements for basement bedrooms require a minimum 5.7 sq ft net clear opening, minimum 24-inch clear height, minimum 20-inch clear width, and a sill no more than 44 inches above the floor. If the well is deeper than 44 inches, a permanent ladder or steps are required. ChatFlow identifies egress compliance gaps for homeowners finishing basements or preparing to sell.
Why do window wells flood and how is it fixed?
Window wells flood when the drainage gravel becomes clogged, preventing water from percolating away. The fix is cleaning or replacing the gravel and drain system, sometimes adding a drainpipe to the footing drain. A fitted polycarbonate cover prevents debris accumulation and reduces maintenance.
