Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help smart home installation companies?
A chatbot qualifies homeowner automation goals 24/7 — identifying whether they want lighting, climate, security, or whole-home integration — and books consultations before competitors respond. It also educates prospects on platform choices (Google Home, Apple HomeKit, Control4, Lutron) reducing pre-sale calls significantly.
What information does the chatbot collect from smart home prospects?
The chatbot captures home size, existing devices (smart speakers, thermostats, locks), desired automation categories, budget range, and preferred timeline — so your sales team arrives with a pre-qualified project scope and can quote with confidence.
Can the chatbot handle questions about specific smart home platforms?
Yes. ChatFlow can be trained with your company's platform expertise — explaining the difference between Z-Wave, Zigbee, and Wi-Fi devices, or why Control4 suits large homes while Google Home works for simpler setups. Prospects get accurate guidance even when your team is off the clock.
