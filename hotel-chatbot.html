Questions hotel owners ask us

Can it handle questions in multiple languages?

Yes. ChatFlow's underlying AI understands and responds in the language the guest writes in. International guests get answers in their native language without any configuration.

Does it integrate with our booking engine?

ChatFlow can explain availability, point guests to your booking engine (Booking.com, direct booking page, etc.) with a link. Full booking engine integration is on our roadmap.

How do I update the information seasonally?

Log into your ChatFlow dashboard and update your bot's training information anytime. Great for seasonal hours, holiday closures, temporary pool closings, or new services.

Will it frustrate guests if it can't answer something?

You control what topics it covers. When a question is outside its scope, it politely suggests calling the front desk or provides your email/phone. Guests never hit a dead end.

What platforms does it work with?

Any website that supports a script tag: WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, Wix, custom HTML. Installation takes under 2 minutes and requires no developer.
