Common questions

Can the chatbot handle same-day delivery questions?

Yes — include your same-day cutoff time, delivery zones, and any same-day surcharges in your system prompt. The bot handles these questions instantly so customers don't call to ask, then buy from a competitor while they wait.

What if a customer wants a custom arrangement?

Configure the bot to collect the occasion, preferred colors, budget, and delivery date — then direct them to call or visit for a custom consultation. The bot qualifies the lead so you spend time on customers who are ready to buy.
