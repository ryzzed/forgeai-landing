Common questions

How much does ChatFlow cost for a personal trainer?

Free plan includes 1,000 conversations/month — more than enough to get started. Starter is $39/month for 5,000 conversations with your own branding. Pro is $79/month for unlimited conversations and multiple bots if you have a studio with other trainers.

Can the chatbot handle nutrition questions too?

Yes. You can configure the bot's system prompt with nutrition guidance information — general principles, whether you offer nutrition coaching, and what your approach covers. Be mindful of regulatory requirements in your jurisdiction around specific nutrition advice.

Will it work on my Squarespace or Wix trainer website?

Yes. ChatFlow works on any website — Squarespace, Wix, WordPress, Webflow, or custom sites. You paste one JavaScript embed code into your site's footer or custom code section.

What if a client asks if I'm certified?

Include your certifications (NASM, ACE, ISSA, CSCS, etc.) and experience in your system prompt and the AI will share that information confidently when asked. You control exactly what the bot knows about your credentials.
