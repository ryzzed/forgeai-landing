Material durability and maintenance education

Wood, aluminum, and vinyl pergolas have very different maintenance profiles — configure ChatFlow to explain this based on the customer's climate. Wood (cedar or pine): natural look, requires staining or sealing every 2–3 years, susceptible to moisture and insects in humid climates. Aluminum: powder-coated, zero maintenance, lasts 30+ years, won't rot, warp, or require painting. Vinyl: low maintenance, won't rot or insects, limited color options. In humid Southern climates, aluminum's maintenance-free durability is often worth the price premium — the chatbot makes this case before the customer defaults to wood on price alone.
