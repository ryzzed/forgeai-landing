Common questions

Should I use Zoho SalesIQ or ChatFlow?

Use Zoho SalesIQ if you're already in the Zoho ecosystem (Zoho CRM, Zoho Desk) and want visitor tracking with agent handoff. Use ChatFlow if you want a standalone AI chatbot that answers questions automatically without requiring live agents. ChatFlow is fully autonomous; SalesIQ is better when you have support staff who respond to chats.

Does ChatFlow integrate with Zoho CRM?

Not directly via a native integration. ChatFlow stores conversation history in its own dashboard. If you need CRM integration, you can manually export or copy lead details. Native Zoho CRM integration is on the roadmap. If CRM sync is critical, evaluate SalesIQ's native Zoho integration versus ChatFlow's simpler AI-first approach.
