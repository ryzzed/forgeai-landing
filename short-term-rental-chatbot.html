Common questions

Can I add this to my Airbnb or VRBO listing?

ChatFlow is a website chat widget — it works on your personal/direct booking website, not inside Airbnb or VRBO platform listings (those platforms don't allow third-party scripts). If you have a direct booking website or a property management website, ChatFlow works perfectly there. For platform-only listings, guests message through Airbnb/VRBO's internal system.

Should I include my actual WiFi password in the chatbot?

For security, we recommend not including the actual password in a publicly-facing chatbot. Instead, configure the bot to give the general location of the password card in the house ("on the kitchen counter by the coffee maker") and tell guests it changes between stays. This way you're still helpful without broadcasting your credentials.

I manage 5 properties — can one chatbot cover all of them?

You can create a separate chatbot for each property with property-specific information, or create one general bot for your portfolio website with each property's details included. ChatFlow supports multiple bots per account (on Starter and Pro plans), so property managers can configure separate bots for different properties with their unique house rules, amenities, and local info.
