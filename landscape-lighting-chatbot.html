Smart home and automation integration

Smart landscape lighting is increasingly expected — configure ChatFlow to ask about smart home platform preferences in the opening question. Smart-capable systems with app control, sunrise/sunset automation, and scene programming add $500–1,500 to the project but dramatically increase customer satisfaction and reduce service calls from customers who forget to turn systems on or off. Customers who use Google Home, Alexa, or Apple HomeKit are natural smart lighting buyers — capturing this interest during the chat ensures the designer arrives with the right equipment for a demo.

Annual service and seasonal add-ons

Professional landscape lighting systems require annual maintenance — bulb and lamp replacement, transformer check, and seasonal adjustments for changing plantings. Configure ChatFlow to mention an annual service agreement ($150–300/year) when booking the installation. Seasonal holiday lighting integration is another natural add-on for landscape lighting customers — they already have a professional transformer installed and trust your company. Annual service customers represent reliable recurring revenue and are first in line for lighting upgrades as their landscape matures.
