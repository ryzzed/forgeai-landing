Common questions

Does ChatFlow have visitor recordings like Smartsupp?

No — visitor session recording is unique to Smartsupp and is useful for e-commerce UX analysis. ChatFlow focuses exclusively on the conversational AI layer. If visitor recording is your primary need, Smartsupp (or Hotjar/Microsoft Clarity for free recording) serves that purpose better. ChatFlow is the right choice when AI chatbot automation is the goal.

Can I use ChatFlow and Smartsupp together?

Technically yes — they're both widget scripts. But running two chat widgets creates a confusing visitor experience. Most businesses choose one: Smartsupp if you have live support agents who respond to chats, ChatFlow if you want AI to handle conversations autonomously without staffing.

How does ChatFlow pricing compare to Smartsupp?

Smartsupp Standard is $19.50/agent/month. For a 2-agent team, that's $39/month — same as ChatFlow Starter. ChatFlow Pro is $79/month for unlimited conversations. Smartsupp Professional is $48/agent/month. The real difference is ChatFlow requires zero agents to run — it's autonomous.
