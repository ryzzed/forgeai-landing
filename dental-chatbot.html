Questions dental practices ask us

Will it give wrong medical advice?

No. You define exactly what topics the bot can answer. Limit it to practice information (hours, insurance, booking, pricing) and it won't venture into clinical advice. For emergencies, it always directs patients to call you directly.

Does it integrate with our booking system?

ChatFlow can link patients to your existing booking page (Zocdoc, your own system, etc.) with a direct URL. Full calendar integration is on our roadmap.

How do I update the information?

Log in to your ChatFlow dashboard, click your bot, and update the training information. Changes go live immediately — no re-deployment needed.

Is patient conversation data private?

Yes. Conversations are stored securely and only accessible to you via your dashboard. We do not share conversation data with third parties. No PHI is collected unless a patient volunteers it.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that lets you add a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, custom HTML, and more. Installation takes under 2 minutes.
