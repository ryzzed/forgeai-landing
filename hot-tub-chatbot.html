Common questions

How much does a hot tub cost installed?

Entry-level portable hot tubs run $4,000–8,000. Mid-range runs $8,000–14,000. Premium brands run $14,000–25,000+. Delivery and electrical hookup add $500–1,500. An in-ground spa runs $15,000–35,000+. The chatbot qualifies budget tier and use case (family size, jets priority, therapy vs entertaining) before routing to the right product category.

How does the chatbot handle electrical requirement questions?

Configure the chatbot to explain upfront: portable hot tubs require a dedicated 240V/50A circuit. If the customer doesn't have outdoor electrical, an electrician runs a line from the panel ($400–700 typically). Customers who learn about this upfront budget for it as part of the project — rather than discovering it after the sale and feeling surprised or deceived.

What add-ons should the chatbot mention?

Configure the chatbot to mention insulated cover ($300–600, essential), cover lifter ($150–350), steps and handrail ($150–300), chemical starter kit, annual service plan, and outdoor lighting or privacy screen integration. These add-ons are mentioned during the research conversation — not as pressure, but as part of the full picture — priming customers to approve them at the showroom.
