Municipal vs well water routing

Municipal and well water customers have fundamentally different needs — configure ChatFlow to ask about water source early. Municipal: typically safe, treated for pathogens, but may have hardness, chlorine, or disinfection byproducts. Softener and carbon filter covers most concerns. Well water: untreated, highly variable — iron, manganese, bacteria, nitrates, or hardness depending on local geology. Route well water customers for comprehensive water testing before recommending a system. Recommending a softener to someone who also has high iron without testing first leads to system failure and customer complaints.
