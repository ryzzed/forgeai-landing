What services are typically included in fall yard cleanup?
Fall cleanup typically includes leaf blowing and removal from lawn and beds, mulching leaves into the lawn, gutter cleaning, bed edging and final mow, and debris hauling. ChatFlow scopes the right service package and gives an accurate price range before the crew arrives.
Should leaves be mulched or removed from the lawn?
Light leaf coverage can be mulched in. Heavy accumulation left through winter causes turf smothering, fungal disease, and pH damage. ChatFlow explains this so homeowners understand removal is maintenance — protecting a lawn investment that costs far more to repair than to protect.
