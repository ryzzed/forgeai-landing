Frequently asked questions

How does ChatFlow respond without live agents?

ChatFlow uses an AI model (LLaMA 3.3 70B) that you configure with a system prompt describing your business, products, and policies. It reads that context and generates accurate answers to visitor questions — no agent training required. Most businesses are live in under 10 minutes.

What if a visitor asks something the AI can't handle?

You can configure your bot to direct complex queries to your email or contact page. The AI handles routine questions (hours, pricing, FAQs, booking info) and gracefully routes edge cases. Over time you can refine the system prompt to handle more scenarios.

Does ChatFlow integrate with my existing tools?

ChatFlow embeds via a JavaScript snippet — it works on any website, WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, or custom stack. Native integrations with CRMs and helpdesks are on the roadmap.

How do I install ChatFlow on my website?

After signing up, copy the one-line embed code from your dashboard and paste it into your site's HTML before the closing </body> tag. If you're on WordPress, use our free plugin from the plugin directory.

What happens when I hit the monthly conversation limit?

The bot shows a friendly message letting visitors know the monthly quota has been reached and links to your site to contact you. You can upgrade at any time to restore service — upgrades take effect immediately.
