Common questions

Can ChatFlow create Freshdesk tickets for escalations?

Not currently — ChatFlow handles conversations fully via AI and logs them in its own dashboard. Freshdesk integration for ticket escalation is on the roadmap. If that integration is critical now, that's a reason to stay with Freshdesk's own chatbot.

Does ChatFlow replace Freshchat (Freshdesk's chat product)?

For AI-only website chat automation, yes. Freshchat also supports live agent chat — ChatFlow doesn't. If you need live agents plus AI, Freshchat's hybrid model has that. ChatFlow is focused on pure AI automation.

How does the AI know what to say about my business?

You write a plain-text description in the bot configuration: services, pricing, hours, policies, FAQs. The AI uses that as its knowledge base and answers questions naturally based on it. No flow builder, no intent categories, no scripted paths.

What happens after the free 1,000 conversation limit?

The bot shows a message that the monthly quota has been reached and links to the upgrade page. No conversations are lost. Upgrade to Starter ($39/month) to continue with 5,000 conversations.
