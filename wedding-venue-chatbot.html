Common questions

Can the chatbot handle elopement or micro-wedding inquiries?

Yes — configure the bot to explain your micro-wedding or elopement packages separately. If you offer weekday or Sunday pricing for smaller ceremonies, include those details so budget-conscious couples have a clear path to booking without thinking you're out of their range.

What if someone asks about specific date availability?

The bot can explain general availability windows and direct couples to contact you for specific date confirmation. Configure it to collect their date preference and contact info so you can follow up with a direct answer — this is the natural conversion flow for venue bookings.
