Common questions

Can the chatbot handle long-distance move inquiries differently from local?

Yes — configure your system prompt to ask about move destination early and provide different pricing guidance for local (hourly) vs long-distance (flat rate estimate) inquiries. The AI adapts its responses based on what the customer tells it.

What if a customer asks about specific items like pianos or safes?

Include your specialty item policies in the system prompt — extra charges for pianos, pool tables, safes, or oversized furniture. The AI will communicate these accurately and flag heavy-item moves for your estimator to handle directly.
