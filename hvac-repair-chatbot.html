Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help HVAC repair companies get more calls?
A chatbot triages AC or furnace symptoms 24/7 -- capturing error codes, symptom descriptions, and equipment age -- then dispatches emergency or scheduled service calls. During heat waves and cold snaps when call volume spikes, a chatbot captures every lead simultaneously while a phone line can only handle one at a time.
What HVAC symptoms can the chatbot triage?
ChatFlow asks about specific symptoms: AC blowing warm air, compressor not running, ice on the unit, furnace clicking but not igniting, error codes on the thermostat, unusual noises, and carbon monoxide detector alerts. Each symptom routes to the right service response -- emergency dispatch, next-day repair, or scheduled maintenance.
Can the chatbot handle repair vs replace recommendations?
Yes. ChatFlow captures equipment age and failure type to apply the standard rule: if repair cost exceeds 50% of replacement cost on equipment over 10 years old, replacement is usually the better investment. This education sets expectations before your technician arrives and reduces objections to replacement quotes.
