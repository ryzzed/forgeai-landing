Can ChatFlow answer questions on behalf of a local business?

Yes. ChatFlow's AI is configured with a system prompt where you describe your business — services, pricing, hours, location, booking process. Visitors get accurate answers immediately, around the clock. It works for any local business vertical.

Is there a long-term commitment with ChatFlow?

No. ChatFlow is month-to-month billing with no annual contract. You can cancel or change plans at any time from your Paddle billing portal. Upgrades take effect immediately.

How do I switch from Podium webchat to ChatFlow?
