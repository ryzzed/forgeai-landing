Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a commercial painting company win more contracts?

A chatbot qualifies building type, square footage, surface condition, and scheduling constraints — so your estimator arrives with a pre-scoped walkthrough. It captures facility managers researching after hours and property management companies with recurring annual repaint contracts, routing them to the right estimator with pre-qualified scope.

What should a commercial painting chatbot ask to pre-qualify leads?

Ask building type and size, interior vs exterior, surface prep needed, scheduling constraints (nights and weekends only, occupied vs vacant), and timeline. Occupied spaces requiring night and weekend work command 20-30% premium pricing — identifying this upfront ensures accurate quotes.

Can the chatbot capture multi-family property management accounts?

Yes. Property management companies with 10+ unit buildings are the highest-value recurring commercial painting accounts. The chatbot identifies property management inquiries by asking about unit count and frequency of repaints — routing these leads to your account manager for a relationship-focused consultation.
