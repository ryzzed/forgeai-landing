Frequently asked questions

Does HubSpot free plan include a chatbot?

HubSpot's free plan includes a live chat widget and basic chatbot "flows" — scripted decision trees where you define the conversation path. AI-generated responses (Breeze AI) require a paid Sales Hub or Service Hub plan starting at $800/month.

Can ChatFlow integrate with HubSpot?

ChatFlow stores all conversations in your dashboard. Native HubSpot CRM sync is on the roadmap. If you need conversations in HubSpot today, you can embed ChatFlow and use Zapier or webhook integrations to forward conversation data.

Is ChatFlow good for lead capture like HubSpot?

ChatFlow's AI can collect visitor information conversationally during a chat session. You can configure the bot to ask for name, email, and inquiry details before answering, effectively capturing leads. Those conversations are logged in your dashboard.

How do I migrate from HubSpot Live Chat to ChatFlow?

Sign up for ChatFlow, create a bot with a system prompt describing your business, remove HubSpot's chat code from your site, and add ChatFlow's one-line embed. The process takes about 10 minutes. Your historical HubSpot conversations remain in HubSpot.
