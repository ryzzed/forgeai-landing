Common questions

When should I use Pure Chat instead of ChatFlow?

Use Pure Chat if you have a dedicated support team or sales team actively monitoring chat during business hours, and your primary goal is live human-to-human conversation. Pure Chat is well-designed for that use case. ChatFlow is better when you want automation — AI that handles inquiries without staffing.

Can ChatFlow do live agent handoff?

Not currently — ChatFlow is designed for fully automated AI conversations, not hybrid AI + live agent workflows. The AI can capture contact details and tell visitors to expect a callback for complex requests, but there's no live agent handoff interface. If agent handoff is essential, evaluate tools that support both AI and live routing.

How does ChatFlow pricing compare to Pure Chat?

ChatFlow Pro is $79/month for unlimited conversations. For small businesses without a dedicated chat team, ChatFlow delivers more at the same price point.
