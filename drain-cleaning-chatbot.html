Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a drain cleaning business get more calls?

An AI chatbot qualifies drain issues 24/7 — distinguishing a slow kitchen drain from a main sewer backup — and books emergency service before the homeowner calls a competitor. It captures jobs at midnight when blockages happen and routes urgent calls to your on-call technician automatically.

What should a drain cleaning chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask which drain is affected (kitchen, bathroom, floor drain, main sewer), whether multiple fixtures are backing up (indicates main line), how long the issue has persisted, and whether there is sewage smell or visible overflow. These answers determine urgency and pricing tier before dispatch.

Can the chatbot handle main sewer line emergencies?

Yes. When a homeowner reports multiple fixtures backing up or sewage smell, the chatbot flags it as a main sewer emergency, collects address and best callback number, and sends your technician an immediate SMS alert — critical for jobs that can cause $10,000+ in water damage if delayed.
