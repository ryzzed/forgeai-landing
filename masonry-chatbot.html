Common questions

How does the chatbot quote paver patio installation?

You configure paver patio price ranges in the system prompt. Concrete pavers: $15–25/sq ft installed (includes base preparation, compacted gravel, sand bed, pavers, polymeric sand, sealer). Natural stone (bluestone, flagstone): $25–45/sq ft installed. Tumbled travertine: $20–35/sq ft. The chatbot asks for patio dimensions and material preference, gives the installed range, and books the free design estimate.

Can the chatbot quote retaining wall construction?

Retaining walls are quoted by linear foot plus wall height. Configure your ranges: segmental block retaining wall $35–55/LF for a 3-foot wall; natural stone dry-stack $50–80/LF; poured concrete with veneer $80–130/LF. Taller walls require engineering review and permits in most jurisdictions. The chatbot asks for approximate length and height, gives a range, and flags taller walls (over 4 feet) for engineering consultation during the estimate.

Does the chatbot handle brick repair and tuckpointing questions?

Yes. Configure pricing in the system prompt: tuckpointing (repointing mortar joints) typically $5–12/sq ft of wall area; brick replacement $15–25 per brick including matching and labor; chimney tuckpointing $300–800 for a typical residential chimney. The chatbot captures the repair description and area, gives a range, and books a site visit for exact scoping.
