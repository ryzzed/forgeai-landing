Common questions

Can the chatbot handle telehealth PT questions?

Yes — if you offer telehealth sessions, configure the bot to explain which conditions are suitable for telehealth versus in-clinic treatment, what the session involves, technical requirements, and whether insurance covers it. Many PT clinics have expanded telehealth offerings and this is a common question from patients in different geographic areas.

What about patients asking if PT will help their specific condition?

Instruct the bot to respond warmly but defer to a clinical evaluation: "I can't say for certain without an evaluation, but [condition] is something we commonly treat — a 45-minute initial evaluation with one of our therapists would give you a proper assessment and clear picture of whether PT is right for your situation. Want to schedule that?" This protects you clinically while still capturing the lead.

Can I configure it for a multi-therapist clinic with different specialties?

Yes — include each therapist's name, certifications, and specialty areas in the system prompt. The bot can then match patient inquiries to the right therapist. "For sports rehab, I'd recommend Dr. [Name] who is a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist and works with a lot of runners and athletes."
