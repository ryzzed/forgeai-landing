Common questions

Can the chatbot check real-time availability?

ChatFlow doesn't connect to your booking calendar directly. It captures the client's preferred dates and contact info, and your team confirms availability during the follow-up call. For direct calendar booking, you'd need a reservation software integration. ChatFlow's value is handling the FAQ and lead capture — not replacing your booking system.

What if someone has a dog breed we don't accept?

Include your breed restriction policy in the system prompt. If your facility doesn't accept certain breeds, the chatbot will politely inform the pet parent upfront — before they come in for an evaluation. This saves both parties time and prevents disappointment at the door.

How is ChatFlow different from just using a contact form?

A contact form is passive — visitors have to decide to fill it out. A chatbot is conversational and proactive: it engages visitors, answers their specific questions, and then naturally asks for their info after providing value. Chatbot lead capture rates are typically 3-5x higher than static contact forms because visitors get their questions answered first.
