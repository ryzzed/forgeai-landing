Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a carpet installation company get more measure appointments?

A chatbot qualifies room count, lifestyle factors (kids, pets, high traffic), and fiber preference — recommending the right carpet grade before your salesperson visits. It captures homeowners triggered by new pet stains, a home sale, or a remodel, and books free measure-and-quote appointments during evening hours when flooring decisions are typically made.

What should a carpet installation chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask number and size of rooms, whether they have pets, children under 12, whether anyone has allergies, and their primary concern — softness, durability, or stain resistance. These answers let you pre-select 2-3 carpet options to bring to the measure, reducing decision time and increasing close rates.

Can the chatbot upsell carpet padding during the booking process?

Yes. After capturing carpet preference, the chatbot explains that premium 8-pound carpet pad doubles the feel and extends carpet life by 30-40% — typically adding $200-$600 to residential jobs. Homeowners who understand the pad role are far more likely to accept the upgrade than those hearing about it for the first time at the sale.
