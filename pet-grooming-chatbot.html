Common questions

Can the chatbot handle cat grooming too?

Yes — include cat grooming services in your system prompt and the AI handles questions about feline grooming packages, lion cuts, bath services, and handling requirements for cats. Just configure it for whatever services you actually offer.

What if someone asks about aggressive or difficult dogs?

Configure your system prompt with your policy on handling difficult dogs — whether you charge extra, require a consultation, or refer out. The AI will communicate your policy accurately and direct them to call you for a conversation if needed.
