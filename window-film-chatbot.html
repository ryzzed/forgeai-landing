Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a window film company get more installations?

A chatbot identifies the primary problem — heat and glare, fading furniture, privacy, or security — and recommends the right film type before your estimator arrives. It captures homeowners complaining about hot rooms and high AC bills and books commercial building managers looking to reduce HVAC loads, all without a phone call.

What should a window film chatbot ask to qualify residential leads?

Ask the primary concern (heat rejection, UV fading protection, privacy, aesthetics), which rooms or windows are affected, and whether they have existing window treatments not solving the problem. High solar-gain rooms with furniture fading are the strongest candidates — the payback on heat-rejection film is typically 3-5 years in energy savings alone.

Can the chatbot handle commercial window film inquiries for office buildings?

Yes. Commercial property managers inquiring about window film typically want HVAC load reduction and glare control for employee productivity. The chatbot collects total glazing square footage, building orientation, and whether this is new installation or replacement — routing commercial inquiries to your dedicated estimator with pre-qualified scope.
