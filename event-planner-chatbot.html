Common questions

I do both weddings and corporate events — can the bot handle both?

Yes — configure separate pricing, typical timelines, and package details for each event type in your system prompt. The bot identifies the type of event the visitor is asking about and adapts its answers accordingly. You can even configure different lead capture prompts (wedding: ask for date and venue status; corporate: ask for company, attendee count, and event objective).

How do I handle the question "are you available on [date]?"

Configure the bot to say "I can't confirm availability in real-time, but send me your date and I'll check our calendar and get back to you within [X] hours." The bot then captures their name, email, and date — giving you a warm lead with all the info needed to follow up quickly.

What about questions about past events and portfolio?

Include descriptions of notable events you've planned in your system prompt — types, scale, venues, themes. The bot can describe your experience and portfolio without you needing to be available at 11pm when couples are browsing. You can also link to your portfolio gallery in the chatbot widget settings.
