Common questions

How does the chatbot quote carpet cleaning by room?

You configure your room-based pricing in the system prompt. A typical structure: first room $75–95, each additional room $35–55, stairs $3–5 per step. Or a flat per-room rate by room type. The chatbot asks how many rooms and any special considerations (heavily soiled, pets), gives a range, and captures the booking with preferred timing.

Can the chatbot upsell Scotchgard and pet treatment?

Yes. After quoting the cleaning, ChatFlow can mention stain protection (Scotchgard or equivalent, $20–30 per room), pet urine treatment ($40–70 per affected area), deodorizer ($15–25 per room), and upholstery cleaning (sofa $80–150, loveseat $60–100). The chatbot mentions these naturally as part of the booking conversation, which increases average job value without feeling pushy.

How do I handle same-day and next-day booking requests?

You configure availability guidance in the system prompt — for example, "we typically have 1–2 day availability" or "same-day service available when requested by 10am." The chatbot sets accurate expectations for turnaround and captures the booking with the customer's preferred timing. Urgent requests can be flagged for priority follow-up in your dashboard.
