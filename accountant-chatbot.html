Questions accounting firms ask us

Will it give tax advice we can't stand behind?

No — you define exactly what the bot covers. Limit it to practice information (services, pricing, intake requirements, deadlines) and it will not give specific tax or legal advice. It always directs complex questions to schedule a consultation with your team.

Can it qualify prospects before a consultation?

Yes. You can train the bot to ask about entity type, revenue range, or specific needs, and guide prospects toward the right service tier — so your consultations start with better-qualified leads.

How do I update the bot during tax season?

Log into your ChatFlow dashboard and update the training information anytime — instantly. Add deadline reminders, change pricing, or adjust availability without touching code.

Is client conversation data secure?

Yes. Conversations are stored securely and only accessible to your firm via the dashboard. The bot is designed for general inquiries, not for collecting sensitive financial data. Prospects should not share SSNs or financial account numbers in chat.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that allows a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, or custom HTML. Setup takes under 2 minutes and requires no developer.
