Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a house cleaning company get more bookings?

A chatbot collects home size, frequency preference, and special requests so your team arrives prepared. It books recurring bi-weekly cleans and captures one-time deep cleans for move-in, move-out, and post-construction situations. Homeowners research cleaning services outside business hours and ChatFlow converts them immediately.

What should a house cleaning chatbot ask to qualify and book leads?

Ask home square footage or bedroom and bathroom count, frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or one-time), whether this is a standard clean or deep clean, if they have pets, any specific focus areas, and preferred day and time. Bi-weekly recurring customers are your most valuable — the chatbot presents recurring options before one-time pricing.

Can the chatbot convert one-time clean customers into recurring clients?

Yes. After booking a one-time deep clean, the chatbot explains the recurring maintenance discount and asks if they would like to schedule ongoing bi-weekly or monthly service. Customers who experience a great deep clean are the most receptive to recurring — capturing that intent during booking is far more effective than asking after the fact.
