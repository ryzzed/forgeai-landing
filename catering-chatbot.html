Common questions

Can the chatbot handle wedding catering inquiries?

Yes — configure the bot with your wedding catering packages, tasting menu process, per-person pricing, and how far in advance to book. Weddings are high-value leads worth collecting carefully — the bot gathers event details, date, and contacts, then your team follows up personally for the consultation.

What if someone asks about a specific menu item or ingredient?

Include sample menus and key ingredients in your system prompt. For detailed ingredient or allergen questions, configure the bot to direct clients to call or email for exact ingredient lists — important for allergy management and appropriate to handle with a human confirmation.
