Questions home service businesses ask us

Can a chatbot handle plumbing or HVAC emergencies?

ChatFlow is configured to recognize emergency language (burst pipe, no heat, flooding) and immediately provide first-response guidance and your emergency contact number. It captures the customer's info so you can call back fast. It does not replace a dispatcher — it captures and triages so your dispatcher's time is better spent.

What types of home service businesses use ChatFlow?

Plumbers, HVAC technicians, electricians, roofers, pest control services, landscapers, general contractors, and other home service businesses. Any business where customers have urgent questions outside business hours benefits from ChatFlow.

Can it give pricing estimates without over-committing?

Yes — you train it with your typical price ranges for common services and your diagnostic/service call fee. It sets expectations without committing to a final price before your tech assesses the job. "Drain cleaning typically runs $150-350 depending on the issue" is useful without being binding.

How do customers contact me after chatting?

The bot collects their name, phone number, and service details. It can also link directly to your scheduling page (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Google Calendar, etc.) for non-emergency appointments.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that accepts a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, or custom HTML. Also works on Google Business Profile via your website link. Setup takes under 2 minutes.
