Common questions

Can the chatbot check real-time desk availability?

ChatFlow doesn't connect to your space management software directly. It can describe your general capacity and current availability based on what you've written in the system prompt, and direct inquiries to your team for real-time confirmation. For live desk inventory, you'd need an integration with your coworking management software.

We offer corporate team packages — can the chatbot handle those?

Yes — include your corporate/team pricing in the system prompt (e.g., team offices starting at X/month for Y desks, enterprise agreements available). The chatbot qualifies team size, collects contact info, and routes larger inquiries to your sales team for a custom quote conversation.

How is a chatbot better than a contact form for a coworking space?

A contact form is passive — the visitor decides to fill it out after reading your website. A chatbot proactively engages, answers their specific questions (What's the WiFi speed? Do you have private call rooms?), and then naturally asks if they'd like to tour. Visitors who get answers to their questions are significantly more likely to convert than those who just fill out a generic form.
