Pricing FAQ

What counts as a conversation?

Each visitor message sent to your bot counts as one conversation. Bot responses don't count. The counter resets on the 1st of each month.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What happens when I hit the monthly limit?

Your bot shows a friendly message letting visitors know the monthly quota has been reached and links to your site to contact you. You can upgrade at any time to restore service.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). Payments are processed securely by Paddle.

Is there an annual plan?

Monthly billing only for now. Annual plans with a discount are on the roadmap.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel from your Paddle billing portal at any time. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
