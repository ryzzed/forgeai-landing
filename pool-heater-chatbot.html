Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help pool heater companies book more installations?
A chatbot qualifies pool size, desired temperature range, usage season, and energy preference — then explains gas vs heat pump vs solar trade-offs so prospects understand operating cost differences before your estimator arrives. Leads from cold pools at swim season start or failed heaters have high urgency and close quickly.
What is the difference between a gas pool heater, heat pump, and solar heater?
Gas heaters heat quickly regardless of air temperature but have high monthly operating costs. Heat pumps are 5x more energy efficient than gas but require air temperatures above 50°F — ideal for warm-climate year-round pools. Solar heaters have minimal operating costs but require adequate roof space and work best in sunny climates. ChatFlow identifies which type fits the customer's pool, climate, and budget.
Can the chatbot handle pool heater repair and replacement leads?
Yes. ChatFlow identifies broken heater symptoms and age to determine whether repair or replacement is the better value. A 12-year-old gas heater with a failed heat exchanger is almost always a replacement. Setting this expectation before the service call prevents surprise up-sells and improves customer satisfaction.
