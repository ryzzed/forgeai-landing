Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help artificial grass companies book more installations?
A chatbot qualifies the use case, square footage, existing surface type, and HOA restrictions — then explains the 10-15 year ROI versus ongoing water, mowing, and fertilizer costs. What are the different artificial grass pile heights and uses?
Putting green turf uses a low 3/8-inch pile for ball roll accuracy. Pet turf uses a medium 1.5-inch pile with drainage backing for odor control. Landscape turf uses a 1.75-2 inch pile for natural appearance. ChatFlow asks about primary use to recommend the right product and backing system.
How does artificial grass handle pet waste and odor?
Pet-grade artificial grass uses a perforated backing with antimicrobial infill to drain liquids and neutralize odor. ChatFlow identifies pet households to recommend pet-specific products with warranty coverage for pet use — a premium upsell that also prevents buyer remorse.
