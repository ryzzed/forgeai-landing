Questions dealerships ask us

Can it tell shoppers about specific vehicles in stock?

Yes, if you add your current inventory to the bot's training information. You can paste your top sellers, key trim levels, and current promotions. For live inventory feeds (real-time stock updates), that's on our roadmap.

Will it promise pricing or rates we can't honor?

You control exactly what the bot says. Keep pricing ranges general ("financing from 3.9% APR for qualified buyers") and it won't create expectations you can't meet. You can also restrict it to topics like test drives and trade-ins only.

What CMS/website platforms does it work with?

Any website that supports a script tag: WordPress, DealerSocket, VinSolutions, CDK, Dealer.com, Squarespace, and custom HTML sites. Your IT team can install it in under 5 minutes.

Can we see what shoppers asked?

Yes. Your ChatFlow dashboard shows every conversation, what questions were asked, and how the bot responded. Great for identifying inventory gaps and training opportunities.

Do we need a developer to set it up?

No. It's one script tag — your service manager or a BDC rep can do it. If your website is managed by a vendor, you can send them the 1-line snippet and ask them to add it to the footer.
