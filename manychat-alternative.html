Common questions

Should I use ManyChat or ChatFlow?

Use ManyChat if your primary goal is automating Instagram/Facebook DMs or running social ad lead funnels through Messenger. Use ChatFlow if your primary goal is converting website visitors — the people who find you on Google and land on your site. Both solve real problems, but for different channels.

Can ChatFlow replace ManyChat for Facebook ads?

No — ManyChat integrates directly with Facebook's Click-to-Messenger ads, which is a specific paid social acquisition channel. ChatFlow doesn't integrate with Messenger. For website traffic conversion, ChatFlow is the right tool. For social ad automation, ManyChat remains the specialist.

How does ChatFlow pricing compare to ManyChat?

ManyChat's Pro plan starts at $15/month for up to 500 contacts but scales to $45/month for 5,000 contacts and higher. ChatFlow is $39/month flat for 5,000 conversations per month regardless of how many unique visitors that represents. For high-traffic websites, ChatFlow's flat pricing wins significantly.
