Common questions

Is ChatFlow useful during slow seasons, not just storms?

Absolutely. Year-round, homeowners research roof age, maintenance, material upgrades, and gutter services. The chatbot handles those questions 24/7 and turns organic website visitors into estimate appointments — storm season or not.

Can I configure the bot for emergency vs. non-emergency responses?

Yes — write different guidance in your system prompt for urgent situations (active leaks, storm damage) vs. general inquiries (replacement planning, material questions). The AI will adapt its tone and urgency accordingly.
