Common questions

When does Kommunicate make more sense than ChatFlow?

If you're a SaaS company building a chatbot into your own product — as a feature for your customers — Kommunicate's SDK and integration architecture is appropriate. If you're a product team that needs to integrate AI chat deeply into a web app, mobile app, or internal tool, Kommunicate's developer-first approach fits that use case. ChatFlow is purpose-built for adding chat to a business website, not embedding chat into a software product.

Can ChatFlow handle the same AI capabilities as Kommunicate?

For the core small business use case — answering questions about your services, qualifying leads, capturing consultation requests — yes. ChatFlow runs LLaMA 3.3 70B and handles complex, natural conversations without any AI configuration on your part. For developers who need to build custom NLP pipelines, fine-tune models, or integrate proprietary knowledge bases via API, Kommunicate offers more flexibility at the cost of significantly more complexity.

How long does it actually take to set up ChatFlow?

Realistically: register (1 minute), write your system prompt describing your business (5-10 minutes), copy the widget script into your website (1-2 minutes). Most users are live within 15 minutes. The system prompt is the only real "work" — and there's no code, no API keys, and no developer dependency at any step.
