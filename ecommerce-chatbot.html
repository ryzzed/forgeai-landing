FAQ

E-commerce chatbot questions

Does it work with Shopify?

Yes. Paste the script tag into your Shopify theme's theme.liquid before the closing </body> tag. Our embed guide has step-by-step instructions. WooCommerce, Squarespace, and Wix are also supported.

Can the bot access my real product catalog?

ChatFlow is trained on text you provide manually — not a live product feed. You write descriptions of your key products, categories, sizing guides, and policies. For stores with large catalogs, the best approach is to train it on FAQs and general product info rather than every SKU.

Can it track orders or check inventory?

Not at this stage. ChatFlow answers questions about your store — policies, products, process — but doesn't connect to live order data or inventory systems. For order tracking, the bot can direct customers to your tracking page or help them find your support email.

How do I keep the bot updated when my policy changes?

Log into the dashboard, edit your bot's training data, and save. Changes go live immediately — no redeployment needed. Most store owners update once a quarter or when policies change.

Will it hurt conversions if the bot gives a wrong answer?

You control what topics the bot can answer via "allowed topics." If someone asks something outside scope, it politely says "I'm not sure about that — here's how to contact support" instead of guessing. No hallucinated answers on out-of-scope questions.
