Common questions

What if I need live agent chat, not just AI?

ChatFlow is AI-only — it's the right tool if you want automation without staffing. If you need a human agent queue, Freshchat or Tidio may be a better fit. That said, most small businesses find that a well-configured AI chatbot handles 80%+ of visitor questions without any humans.

Does ChatFlow work with my existing CRM?

ChatFlow doesn't require a CRM — it captures conversation data in your dashboard. For CRM integration, the bot can be configured to collect visitor details and you can use Zapier or webhooks to push leads to your CRM. Native CRM integration is on the roadmap.

How does the free plan compare to Freshchat's trial?

Freshchat's free trial is time-limited. ChatFlow's free plan is permanent — 1,000 conversations every month with no expiry. You can build and run a real chatbot on the free tier indefinitely, with no credit card required.

Is ChatFlow good for e-commerce stores?

Yes — ChatFlow works on any website including Shopify, WooCommerce, and custom stores. Configure it with your product FAQs, shipping policy, and return process. The AI handles customer questions 24/7 without you adding support staff.
