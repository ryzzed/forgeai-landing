How much does stamped concrete cost per square foot?

Standard stamped patterns run $12–22/sq ft installed. Complex or multi-color work runs $18–28/sq ft. A 400 sq ft patio runs $5,000–9,000. A two-car driveway (600–700 sq ft) runs $8,000–16,000. Compared to pavers at $18–35/sq ft or natural stone at $25–50/sq ft, stamped concrete offers a similar look at significantly lower cost.
