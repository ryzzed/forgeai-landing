FAQ

Restaurant chatbot questions

Do I need a developer to set this up?

No. If you can copy-paste text, you can set up ChatFlow. For WordPress, we have a one-click plugin — no code at all. Most restaurant owners are done in under 5 minutes.

How does the bot know about my menu?

You type it in during setup. Describe your dishes, prices, specials, dietary info — as much or as little as you like. The bot is trained on exactly what you provide. You can update it anytime, and changes go live instantly.

What if a customer asks something the bot doesn't know?

You configure "allowed topics" so the bot stays focused on restaurant questions. If someone asks something outside scope, the bot politely redirects them to call or contact you directly — no hallucinated answers.

Can it take reservations?

ChatFlow handles the information side — hours, availability, how to book, your booking link. Direct table booking integration is on our roadmap. For now, the bot directs customers to your booking system (OpenTable, Resy, your phone, etc.).

What websites does it work on?

Any website that supports adding custom HTML — WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, plain HTML. We have step-by-step guides for each platform at our embed guide.
