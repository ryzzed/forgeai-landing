Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a basement finishing company win more projects?

A chatbot qualifies moisture history, square footage, and intended use (home office, bedroom, entertainment room) so your designer arrives with a focused concept rather than a blank consultation. It books appointments before homeowners request three competing quotes and identifies moisture problems that need waterproofing first.

What should a basement finishing chatbot ask to pre-qualify leads?

Ask about moisture history (any water intrusion, current humidity, musty smell), square footage, ceiling height, current state (unfinished, partial, framed), intended use rooms, and rough budget. Moisture history is the critical filter — a wet basement needs waterproofing before finishing, and discovering this upfront prevents costly callbacks.

Can the chatbot upsell waterproofing before the finishing project?

Yes. When a homeowner mentions past water seepage, current musty smell, or efflorescence on walls, the chatbot explains that finishing over an unaddressed moisture problem voids most warranties and typically causes mold within 2-3 years — booking a combined waterproofing-plus-finishing assessment.
