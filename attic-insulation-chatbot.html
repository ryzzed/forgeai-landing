Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help an attic insulation company get more jobs?

A chatbot qualifies homeowners by asking about high energy bills, drafty rooms, and current attic R-value — then explains exactly how blown-in insulation to R-49 can cut heating and cooling costs 15-25%. It captures utility rebate seekers who need same-season installs and books assessments without requiring a phone call.

What should an attic insulation chatbot ask to pre-qualify leads?

Ask about current energy bills (especially summer cooling costs), whether they notice hot or cold rooms despite HVAC running, the age of the home, and whether they have had an energy audit. These answers identify the best candidates — typically homes built before 1990 with R-11 to R-19 attic insulation that need upgrading to R-38 or R-49.

Can the chatbot help homeowners claim utility rebates for insulation?

Yes. The chatbot identifies rebate-eligible customers by confirming their utility provider and state, explains available rebates (often $150-$500 from utilities plus 30% federal tax credit under the Inflation Reduction Act), and creates urgency around rebate deadlines — converting homeowners who were just researching into booked appointments.
