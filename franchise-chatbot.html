Common questions

Can the franchisor control the chatbot template centrally?

Currently, each franchise location manages their own ChatFlow account, which gives franchisees autonomy while requiring the franchisor to distribute a template. Many franchise systems share a master system prompt document that franchisees copy and customize with their location details. This approach keeps brand standards high without requiring corporate infrastructure. Future multi-account management features are on our roadmap.

What franchise types benefit most from a chatbot?

Service franchises with appointment-based models benefit the most — hair salons, fitness studios, tutoring centers, cleaning services, pet care, auto services. These businesses have predictable FAQ loads (pricing, availability, what to expect) and high value per booked appointment. Retail franchises benefit from reducing location/hours questions that don't need human staff to answer. Any franchise where customers compare multiple locations before choosing is a strong fit.

We're the franchisor — can we offer ChatFlow as a value-add to our franchisees?

Yes — many franchisors recommend or require specific tech tools as part of their system. If you want to standardize ChatFlow across your franchise network, reach out through our website to discuss volume arrangements and onboarding support for your franchisees. A master template that you've already built and tested removes the setup friction for new franchisees entirely.
