Common questions

Can the chatbot quote different vehicle sizes?

Yes. You configure pricing tiers by vehicle class — sedan, SUV/crossover, truck, full-size SUV, van — and the chatbot applies the right rate based on what the customer tells it. A full detail on a sedan might be $180–220 while the same service on a large SUV runs $240–300. The chatbot handles this automatically.

What if a customer asks about paint correction or ceramic coating?

You include your add-on services in the system prompt — paint correction ($150–400 depending on severity), ceramic coating ($500–1,200), paint protection film, headlight restoration, odor elimination. The chatbot explains what each service does, gives price ranges, and captures a contact for higher-ticket consultations that benefit from a personal call.

How does the chatbot handle fleet or commercial accounts?

You can instruct the chatbot to recognize fleet inquiries (3+ vehicles, company name mentioned) and route them to your fleet pricing conversation — typically 10–20% volume discounts, monthly billing, dedicated scheduling windows. The chatbot captures the fleet size, vehicle types, and contact info so you can follow up with a custom proposal.
