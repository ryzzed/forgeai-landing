Common questions

When should I use JivoChat instead of ChatFlow?

Use JivoChat if you have a dedicated support or sales team actively monitoring chat during business hours across multiple channels (web, WhatsApp, Facebook, phone). JivoChat excels at consolidating multi-channel human support. Use ChatFlow when you want 24/7 AI automation and don't want to staff a chat team.

Does ChatFlow support multiple channels like JivoChat?

ChatFlow is currently focused on website chat via an embed script. Multi-channel support (WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger) is on the roadmap. If you need to consolidate WhatsApp and website chat today, JivoChat's multi-channel capability is a genuine differentiator over ChatFlow's current website-only focus.

How does ChatFlow pricing compare to JivoChat?

JivoChat Professional is $19/agent/month ($13/agent billed annually). For a 3-agent team on monthly billing that's $57/month. ChatFlow Starter is $39/month flat — same coverage for fewer agents, or a better deal for larger teams. And ChatFlow requires zero agents to operate, so even the "1 agent" comparison is misleading: ChatFlow eliminates the agent requirement entirely.
