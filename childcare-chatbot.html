Questions childcare centers ask us

Can it answer questions specific to our curriculum or philosophy?

Yes. You train the bot with whatever information you want it to share — your Montessori/Reggio/play-based approach, specific programs, daily routine, and learning philosophy. It answers exactly what you've taught it.

What if a parent asks something we haven't covered?

You control the scope. When a question falls outside the bot's training, it politely suggests calling or emailing the center. Parents never hit a dead end — they're always directed to the right next step.

Can we update information when our schedule changes seasonally?

Yes — log into your ChatFlow dashboard and update anytime. Summer hours, holiday closures, new enrollment dates: changes go live instantly with no developer needed.

Is parent conversation data private?

Yes. Conversations are stored securely and only visible to your center via the dashboard. No children's names or personal data should be shared in chat — the bot is designed for general pre-enrollment inquiries.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that supports a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, or custom HTML. Setup takes under 2 minutes and requires no technical knowledge.
