Common questions

Should I give exact prices in the chatbot?

Price ranges work better than exact quotes for orthodontic treatment, since costs depend on case complexity that can't be assessed without an exam. "Traditional braces typically range from $4,500-6,500 depending on treatment complexity" is honest and useful without over-committing. It qualifies patients who are in your price range and filters out those who aren't — saving everyone time.

Can the chatbot handle adult orthodontic inquiries separately?

Yes — configure your adult orthodontic services, including any adult-specific options (clear aligners, ceramic braces), the fact that it's never too late for treatment, and any adult-specific concerns (like treatment time fitting around professional life). Adult orthodontics is a fast-growing segment and deserves specific treatment in your chatbot configuration.

We offer both orthodontic and general dental services — can the chatbot handle both?

Yes — configure both service areas in your system prompt with clear context about what each involves. The bot can distinguish between orthodontic inquiries (braces, aligners) and general dental questions (cleanings, fillings) and provide appropriate information for each. Many orthodontist-dentist combo practices find the chatbot particularly useful for routing inquiries to the right service line.
