Common questions

Can the chatbot compare pressure-treated wood vs composite vs PVC decking?

Yes. You configure material comparisons in the system prompt. Pressure-treated wood: most affordable ($15–25/sq ft installed), requires annual sealing/staining, natural look. Composite (Trex, TimberTech): mid-range ($25–40/sq ft installed), minimal maintenance, 25-year fade warranty, won't splinter. PVC/cellular PVC: premium ($35–50/sq ft installed), completely impervious to moisture and insects, best for coastal or high-moisture environments. The chatbot sets expectations before the consultation.

How does the chatbot quote a new deck by square footage?

You configure installed price ranges by material and complexity. A standard ground-level PT wood deck: $15–25/sq ft. Composite deck with stairs: $28–42/sq ft. Elevated deck with railings, stairs, and built-in seating: $35–55/sq ft. The chatbot asks for dimensions, deck height, desired features, and material preference, then gives a range and books the design consultation for exact pricing.

Can the chatbot handle deck replacement vs repair questions?

Homeowners with aging decks often aren't sure whether to repair or replace. You configure guidance in the system prompt: if more than 30% of boards need replacing, the structure is compromised, or the deck is over 20 years old, replacement is typically more cost-effective. The chatbot asks about condition and age, gives honest repair vs replace guidance, and captures the lead for an in-person assessment.
