Common questions

Will the chatbot give wrong pricing and get me in trouble?

Configure your bot with price ranges ("starting at") and a disclaimer that final pricing requires a vehicle inspection. This is how shops typically quote anyway — the AI presents the same way. You control the exact language in your system prompt.

Can it handle questions about whether a car is ready for pickup?

The AI can explain your status communication process (how you notify customers) but can't check live repair order status. For status updates, the bot can direct customers to call the shop or check a status link if your shop management software provides one.

How much does ChatFlow cost for a mechanic shop?

Free plan: 1,000 conversations/month, one bot, with ChatFlow branding. Starter: $39/month for 5,000 conversations, no branding. Pro: $79/month for unlimited conversations and up to 5 bots if you have multiple locations.

Does it work on Google Business websites?

ChatFlow embeds on websites where you can add JavaScript — your shop's main website, WordPress, Wix, etc. Google Business profiles don't support custom embeds. Use ChatFlow on your main website and link your Google listing to it.
