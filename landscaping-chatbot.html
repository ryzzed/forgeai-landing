Common questions

Can the chatbot handle commercial property inquiries?

Yes — configure the bot to handle both residential and commercial inquiries. For commercial jobs, have it collect property type, square footage, and desired service frequency so you can scope the job before the site visit.

What if someone asks for an exact price without seeing the property?

Configure the bot to explain that exact pricing requires a free site visit, then offer to collect their info for a free estimate. This is honest, sets proper expectations, and is the natural conversion point for landscaping leads.
