California ADU law expertise capture

California has the most permissive ADU laws in the country — configure ChatFlow to specifically address California homeowners with confidence. Junior ADUs (under 500 sqft attached), standard ADUs, and garage conversions are all streamlined under SB 9 and related legislation. Owner-occupancy requirements were eliminated. Processing time is capped at 60 days. Many California homeowners are unaware of how easy ADU permitting now is — the chatbot that corrects this misconception and books the consultation is converting customers who thought the process was prohibitively complex.
