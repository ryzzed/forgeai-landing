Common questions

When would I choose Landbot over ChatFlow?

Landbot is better for guided intake flows where you need users to answer specific questions in a specific order — like a mortgage calculator, a product configurator, or a structured lead intake form. If your goal is guided data collection through a decision tree, Landbot's flow builder is purpose-built for that. ChatFlow is better for open-ended visitor support and FAQ handling.

Does ChatFlow integrate with CRMs and email tools like Landbot does?

ChatFlow focuses on the conversation layer — AI responses, lead capture, and conversation history. Landbot has more native integrations with CRMs and tools like HubSpot and Zapier. If deep CRM integration is your priority, evaluate both for your specific stack. ChatFlow's conversation history is accessible from the dashboard for manual follow-up.
