Common questions

When should I use Re:amaze instead of ChatFlow?

Use Re:amaze if you have a small support team (2-5 people) and need to centralize customer communication across email, chat, Instagram, and Facebook in one inbox. Re:amaze excels at making a small team more efficient across multiple channels. Use ChatFlow when you want the website chat channel fully automated with no agent involvement.

Can I use ChatFlow alongside Re:amaze?

Technically you could run ChatFlow for website chat while Re:amaze handles email and social. Some businesses do this to reduce the volume of tickets that reach the Re:amaze inbox. However, having two chat interfaces on one website creates a confusing experience. Most businesses choose one channel tool for website chat.
