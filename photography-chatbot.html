Common questions

What if someone asks about a session type I don't offer?

Configure your system prompt to only cover services you offer. If someone asks about something outside your scope (e.g., commercial product photography if you only do portraits), the AI will politely explain your focus and redirect them to what you do offer.

Can the chatbot take deposits or collect payment?

ChatFlow handles conversation and lead capture — payment collection happens through your invoicing system (HoneyBook, Dubsado, PayPal, etc.). Configure the bot to direct clients to your booking link or email after collecting their information.
