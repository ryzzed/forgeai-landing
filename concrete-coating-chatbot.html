What is the difference between polyaspartic and epoxy floor coatings?
Polyaspartic coatings cure in hours, resist UV yellowing, and bond stronger to concrete than standard epoxy. Traditional epoxy takes 3-5 days to cure, yellows in sunlight, and can peel if moisture vapor is present. ChatFlow explains this so prospects understand why professional polyaspartic installations outperform big-box store epoxy kits.
