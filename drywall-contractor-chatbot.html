Full room finishing (hang, tape, mud, sand, texture): $1.50–3.00 per sq ft. New construction drywall: $1.25–2.50 per sq ft for hang and finish. The chatbot asks which type of work the customer needs and routes to the right pricing guidance.

Can the chatbot handle water damage drywall inquiries?

Water damage drywall is a common and urgent inquiry. You configure the chatbot to ask about moisture source (is it fixed?), extent of damage, and whether mold is visible. The chatbot explains that wet or mold-affected drywall needs full replacement rather than patching, gives a rough sq ft range, and captures the lead with urgency context. If the source isn't fixed yet, the chatbot notes that drywall work follows after the leak is resolved.

Does the chatbot handle texture matching questions?

Texture matching is one of the biggest concerns homeowners have about drywall repair. You configure the chatbot to explain your texture matching process — orange peel, knockdown, smooth, skip trowel, popcorn removal/replacement — and note that perfect matching depends on existing texture age and paint. The chatbot captures the texture type and can request a photo via the contact form to assess before quoting.
