Common questions

Can the chatbot help identify pests from descriptions?

Yes — configure the bot with descriptions of the common pests you treat, their appearance, and where they're typically found. The AI can help narrow down what a homeowner is likely dealing with based on their description, then recommend a free inspection to confirm and quote treatment.

What if someone has an emergency (wasps, bees, heavy infestation)?

Include emergency handling instructions in your system prompt: "For active wasp nests, bee swarms, or severe infestations, please call us directly at [number] for same-day emergency service." The bot escalates these appropriately without treating every call as an emergency.
