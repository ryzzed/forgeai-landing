Common questions

How do I add photos of my work to the chatbot?

ChatFlow's chatbot is text-based — it can describe your artists' styles, link to your portfolio (Instagram, website gallery), and explain what types of work each artist specializes in. For a visual portfolio, the chatbot can direct visitors to your Instagram or gallery page with a direct link.

Can the chatbot give accurate custom pricing?

It can give accurate ranges based on what you train it with — hourly rate, minimum, typical ranges for small/medium/large pieces. The chatbot should be clear that final pricing requires a consultation. This sets realistic expectations and reduces no-shows from sticker shock.

My shop has 3 artists with different styles — can the chatbot handle that?

Yes — describe each artist in your system prompt: their specialties (Japanese, fine line, traditional, realism), current availability, booking lead time, and consultation process. The chatbot will match inquiries to the right artist and capture their contact info for the appropriate person to follow up.
