The chatbot asks for lot size or zone count estimate and gives a range, capturing the lead for an on-site measurement and exact quote.

Can the chatbot book seasonal services like spring startups and fall winterizations?

Yes — and seasonal services are ideal for chatbot capture because they're predictable, high-volume, and time-sensitive. Configure your spring startup price range ($75–125 for most residential systems) and fall blowout/winterization ($80–130). The chatbot captures name, address, and preferred timing. How does the chatbot handle broken head or controller repair inquiries?

Repair inquiries are common — broken head, zone not working, controller malfunction, soggy spot in the lawn. Configure the chatbot to collect symptom description, number of affected zones, and system age, then capture a service call booking with a diagnostic fee range ($65–95 trip charge, applied toward repair). This captures the repair lead before the homeowner calls another company.
