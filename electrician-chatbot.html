Common questions

Can the chatbot handle emergency service requests?

Yes — configure your system prompt to include your emergency line number and availability. The AI can identify urgent requests (power outages, sparking outlets, tripped breakers that won't reset) and immediately provide your emergency contact, ensuring no urgent job goes unanswered.

Will the AI give wrong pricing information?

The chatbot only shares what you tell it. Write your pricing as ranges with caveats ("pricing varies based on panel condition and cable run") and the AI will communicate that accurately. It won't invent numbers it wasn't given.

How much does ChatFlow cost for an electrician?

Free plan: 1,000 conversations/month. Starter: $39/month for 5,000 conversations without ChatFlow branding. Pro: $79/month for unlimited conversations. One captured job from a chatbot lead pays for years of subscription.

Can it collect photos of the job site?

The current version handles text conversations. For photo collection, configure the bot to ask for a description and direct customers to email photos to your business address. Native image upload support is on the roadmap.
