Common questions

When should I use Botpress instead of ChatFlow?

Use Botpress if you're a developer who needs deep customization, custom integrations with internal APIs, complex multi-turn conversation flows, or an open-source solution you can modify and self-host. Botpress is excellent for technical teams building sophisticated chatbot systems. ChatFlow is for non-technical business owners who want a working AI chatbot in 2 minutes.

Does ChatFlow have any developer API?

The embed script uses simple `data-bot-id` attributes for configuration — friendly for developers who need to deploy it at scale. A full developer API is on the roadmap. For complex custom integrations, Botpress or an API-first solution may serve better until ChatFlow's API is available.

How does ChatFlow pricing compare to Botpress?

Botpress Cloud has a free tier and usage-based paid plans that can range from affordable to expensive depending on message volume and features. Self-hosting Botpress is free but requires your own infrastructure costs and developer time. ChatFlow is $39/month flat for 5,000 conversations, fully hosted with no infrastructure or maintenance costs.
