How does an AI chatbot help an awning company book more installations?

A chatbot qualifies application, projection size, and motorized vs manual preference — then explains how a 14-foot projection awning can reduce interior temperatures by 15 degrees. What should an awning installation chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask the application (deck retractable, patio fixed, window sun shade, commercial storefront), approximate width needed, primary motivation (shade, UV protection, rain coverage, aesthetics), and whether they want motorized with wind sensor or manual. Motorized awnings represent 60-70% of residential sales and a $500-$1,200 premium over manual.

Yes. Commercial storefront awnings are a distinct product category with fabric, aluminum, or standing-seam metal options and signage printing. The chatbot identifies commercial inquiries, collects storefront width and mounting surface, and routes them to your commercial estimator with full scope.
