What it handles for cleaning businesses
💰

Pricing questions

Shares your pricing structure — flat rate, per-room, or hourly — and typical price ranges based on home size and cleaning type. Sets expectations before prospects call and reduces "just checking prices" calls.

📋

Quote request capture

Collects the details you need to prepare a quote: home size, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, cleaning frequency, special requests, and contact information — so you can follow up with an informed quote.

🔄

Service types explained

Explains the difference between standard cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out, and recurring service. Helps prospects understand what they actually need before they ask for a price.

🧪

Products & supplies

Answers questions about the cleaning products you use, whether you bring supplies or use the client's, eco-friendly or pet-safe options, and any products you avoid — questions that matter to careful buyers.

📅

Availability & scheduling

Explains your availability windows, lead time for first booking, and how recurring scheduling works — and links to your booking system (Jobber, HouseCall Pro, Square, etc.) for confirmed appointments.

🏠

Service area

Tells visitors which zip codes and neighborhoods you serve so out-of-area requests don't clog your pipeline — and confirms coverage for prospects in your area.

Questions cleaning businesses ask us

Can the chatbot give price quotes?

ChatFlow shares your pricing structure and typical ranges (e.g., "standard cleaning for a 3-bed/2-bath home typically runs $120–$180") and collects the prospect's details for an accurate follow-up quote. It doesn't commit to a final price before you've assessed the job.

What types of cleaning businesses use ChatFlow?

Residential maid services, house cleaning companies, Airbnb and vacation rental cleaners, commercial office cleaning services, and move-in/move-out specialists. Any cleaning business with a website benefits from 24/7 quote capture.

Can it handle recurring booking requests?

Yes — train it with your recurring service options (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly), how recurring clients are scheduled, and what discounts apply. It captures the lead and directs them to your booking system for a confirmed appointment.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that accepts a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, or custom HTML. Also works alongside Jobber, HouseCall Pro, or any other booking software — it feeds leads in, not replaces them.
