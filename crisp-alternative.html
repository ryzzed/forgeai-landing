Switching from Crisp?

What Crisp features does ChatFlow not have?

ChatFlow doesn't have multi-channel inbox (email, WhatsApp, Instagram), live chat agent routing, proactive messaging campaigns, CRM contact profiles, or shared team inbox. It's focused: AI that answers website visitor questions automatically. If you need those features, Crisp is worth the price.

How does ChatFlow train its AI for my specific business?

You write a plain-text description of your business in the bot configuration: services, hours, pricing, policies, common questions. The AI uses that as its knowledge base. No knowledge base articles to structure, no flow builder, no intent categories.

Can I run ChatFlow and Crisp at the same time?

Yes — both use script tags and don't conflict. Try ChatFlow on a specific page or section of your site to compare conversation quality before switching fully.

Does ChatFlow work on WordPress and Shopify?

Yes. Any website that accepts a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Shopify, or custom HTML. No plugin required for most platforms.
