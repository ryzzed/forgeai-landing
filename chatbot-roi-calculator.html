How the calculator works

Conservative industry benchmarks, not inflated projections

Engagement rate (12% default)

Research shows 8-20% of website visitors interact with a proactively placed chat widget. The 12% default is a conservative mid-range estimate for a small business site.

Lead capture rate (25% default)

Of visitors who open the chat, roughly 20-35% will provide a name and contact info when asked naturally during the conversation. The 25% default is mid-range.

Close rate (20% default)

How often your business converts an inbound lead into a paying customer. Varies widely: 10-15% for cold traffic, 25-40% for warm/qualified leads. Adjust to your reality.

Customer value ($300 default)

The average revenue per customer. For a restaurant this might be $80 (recurring visits). For a law firm it might be $2,000+. Change this to your average transaction or lifetime value.

Common questions

These numbers seem optimistic — is the calculator realistic?

The defaults are conservative mid-range estimates based on industry benchmarks. Your actual results depend heavily on your site traffic quality, chatbot configuration, and how well the system prompt matches what your visitors are asking about. A well-configured chatbot for a relevant query (e.g. a dental chatbot for someone searching "dentist near me") will outperform the defaults. A generic chatbot on low-intent traffic will underperform. Use this as a directional estimate, not a guarantee.

What if my average sale is very low (like a coffee shop)?

For low-ticket businesses, the ROI math requires either high volume or lifetime value. A coffee shop chatbot might capture loyalty program signups — each worth $200-400 over a year. Use your annual customer lifetime value instead of single transaction value for a more accurate picture.

Does the calculator include cost savings from automation?

No — this calculator only models revenue from new leads. The full ROI picture also includes: time saved answering repetitive questions (if you currently respond to chats yourself), reduced need for a live chat agent, and after-hours coverage that would otherwise be lost revenue. The actual ROI is often higher than what the calculator shows.
