How does the chatbot explain mold remediation costs?

Mold remediation costs vary widely by affected area and scope. Configure your typical ranges: small surface mold (bathroom tile, under 10 sq ft) $300–700; moderate growth behind drywall (one wall or room) $1,500–4,000; extensive growth spanning multiple areas $5,000–15,000+. Attic mold treatment runs separately ($2,000–5,000 for average attic). The chatbot gives these ranges and emphasizes that an inspection is required for accurate scoping and pricing.

Can the chatbot handle insurance claim mold questions?

Insurance coverage for mold varies by policy and cause. Configure the chatbot to explain that mold from a sudden covered event (burst pipe, roof leak from storm) is often covered; mold from long-term moisture or neglect typically isn't. You can note that you provide documentation for insurance claims and work with adjusters. Capturing whether the customer has an open insurance claim is useful context for your team when they follow up.
