Questions mortgage professionals ask us

Can the chatbot give interest rate quotes?

ChatFlow explains rate factors and captures the prospect's details for a personalized quote from a licensed loan officer. It sets expectations without making rate commitments — those require a licensed conversation and current market data.

Does it comply with mortgage advertising regulations (TRID, RESPA)?

ChatFlow is a lead capture and education tool — not an advertisement for specific loan terms. Configure it to explain general loan concepts and capture inquiry information. Consult your compliance officer about specific disclosures for your market.

Can it handle both purchase and refinance leads?

Yes. Train it to handle both intent types — identifying whether the visitor is buying, refinancing, or just researching — and route each conversation appropriately toward your application or consultation booking.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that accepts a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, or custom HTML. Also works on independently hosted loan officer pages and team sites.
