Questions salons ask us

What if a client asks something the bot doesn't know?

You define exactly what topics the bot covers. If a client asks something outside its knowledge, it politely says it can't answer and suggests they call or book directly. You're always in control of what it says.

Can it take bookings directly?

ChatFlow can explain your services and direct clients to your existing booking system (Vagaro, Mindbody, Fresha, Booksy, or a custom URL). Full booking integration is on our roadmap for 2026.

How do I update pricing or add a new service?

Log into your ChatFlow dashboard, click your bot, and update the information. Changes are instant — no re-deploying or contacting support.

Does it match our salon's branding?

Yes. You set the accent color to match your brand. On the Starter plan you remove ChatFlow branding entirely. Pro includes full white-label with custom bot name and colors.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that allows a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Vagaro website builder, custom HTML. Setup takes under 2 minutes.
