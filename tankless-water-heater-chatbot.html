Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help tankless water heater companies book more jobs?

What information does the chatbot collect for tankless water heater quotes?
The chatbot captures number of bathrooms, simultaneous hot water usage, current water heater type and age, gas line size availability, and whether the homeowner wants whole-home or point-of-use installation. This pre-qualification prevents undersizing mistakes and lets installers provide accurate quotes on the first call.
Can the chatbot identify when a tank water heater needs urgent replacement?
Yes. ChatFlow asks about rust-colored water, rumbling or popping noises, water pooling around the base, inconsistent hot water, and heater age over 10 years -- classic signs of imminent failure. Homeowners with these symptoms get routed to same-week installation scheduling to prevent a complete failure and potential water damage.
