Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help home security companies get more clients?
A chatbot captures leads in high-urgency moments — right after a neighborhood break-in or during a new home move-in — when homeowners search at 11pm. It qualifies their property type, monitoring preferences, and camera needs, then books a free security assessment before the emotion fades.
Can the chatbot handle questions about monitoring contracts and pricing?
Yes. ChatFlow can explain the difference between no-contract and 24-month monitoring plans, professional monitoring fees, and equipment ownership models — filtering out price-shoppers and qualifying serious buyers for your sales team.
What triggers homeowners to contact a security company?
The top triggers are: a neighbor's home was burglarized, the family just moved into a new home, a door or window was damaged, or a teenager is home alone regularly. ChatFlow is trained to recognize these trigger scenarios and respond with urgency-appropriate messaging to maximize conversions.
