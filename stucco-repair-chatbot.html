Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a stucco repair company win more jobs?

A chatbot triages crack type and moisture exposure, creates urgency around water infiltration risk, and captures homeowners who noticed damage after a rain event. It explains the difference between traditional three-coat stucco and EIFS synthetic systems and books inspections before water damage reaches the sheathing.

What should a stucco repair chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask crack pattern (hairline diagonal, stair-step at corners, horizontal runs), whether there is water staining or moisture inside near the cracks, stucco type (traditional or EIFS), home age, and whether any painting or caulking has been applied over the cracks. Each answer narrows scope and identifies whether this is cosmetic or moisture remediation.

Can the chatbot identify when stucco cracks indicate serious structural or water problems?

Yes. Stair-step cracks at corners are a red flag for foundation movement. Horizontal cracks below windows frequently indicate missing flashing and active water infiltration. The chatbot identifies these patterns, explains the risk of delay, and creates urgency for an immediate inspection appointment.
