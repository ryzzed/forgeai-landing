Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help chimney repair companies book more jobs?
A chatbot identifies visible damage symptoms — spalling brick, crumbling mortar, white staining, water in the firebox — then explains the water infiltration and structural risks of deferring repairs. ChatFlow creates informed urgency and books the inspection before an ignored $800 tuckpointing job becomes a $12,000 rebuild.
What are the most common chimney repair problems?
The most common issues are tuckpointing (failed mortar joints), spalling brick (freeze-thaw damaged face brick), crown repair (cracked concrete crown), flashing failure (water leaking at the roof-chimney intersection), and flue liner damage (cracked tiles creating fire and CO hazard). ChatFlow asks targeted questions to identify the repair category before the inspection visit.
Can the chatbot identify when a chimney needs tuckpointing vs full rebuild?
Yes. ChatFlow asks about mortar joint depth loss, stair-step cracking patterns, chimney lean, and interior water staining to triage between tuckpointing, partial rebuild, and full teardown-rebuild. Setting scope expectations early reduces estimate no-shows and surprise rejections.
