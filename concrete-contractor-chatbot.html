Common questions

How does the chatbot quote concrete driveways and patios?

You configure your installed price ranges by project type in the system prompt. Typical ranges: standard broom-finish driveway $6–10/sq ft installed; decorative stamped concrete patio $12–20/sq ft; colored concrete $8–12/sq ft; basic patio slab $5–8/sq ft. The chatbot asks for dimensions or an estimate and gives a realistic range, noting that exact pricing requires a site visit for subbase assessment and slope evaluation.

Can the chatbot handle decorative concrete and stamped concrete questions?

Yes. You configure decorative options in the system prompt — stamped patterns (ashlar slate, cobblestone, wood plank), integral color, acid stain, exposed aggregate, broom finish. The chatbot explains each option, gives the price premium over standard concrete, and captures the lead for a consultation where you show samples. Decorative concrete jobs command significantly higher margins and are worth capturing before the customer calls a standard paving company.

Does the chatbot handle driveway removal and replacement vs overlay?

Customers often ask whether they need full replacement or if an overlay is possible. You configure the guidance in the system prompt: overlays work on structurally sound concrete with minor surface issues; cracked, heaving, or crumbling concrete typically requires full removal and replacement (add $2–4/sq ft for demo and haul-off). The chatbot explains the distinction and notes that you assess condition during the free estimate.
