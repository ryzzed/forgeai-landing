Common questions

Does ChatFlow replace Intercom's AI chatbot (Fin)?

Yes, for the chatbot use case specifically. ChatFlow replaces Fin as the AI that answers visitor questions on your website. It doesn't replace Intercom's ticketing, inbox, or live chat agent tools.

Can I run ChatFlow alongside Intercom?

Yes — some businesses use ChatFlow on their primary website and keep Intercom for internal support workflows. You can also run ChatFlow for 30 days to compare engagement before canceling Intercom.

Does ChatFlow have a free trial?

The free plan is effectively a permanent free trial — 1,000 conversations per month at $0 with no time limit. No credit card needed to start.

Can ChatFlow handle the same questions Intercom's bot handles?

For FAQ-style business questions (services, pricing, hours, booking, policies) — yes. For complex ticket routing, CRM field updates, or product tour triggers — those are Intercom-specific capabilities ChatFlow doesn't replicate.
