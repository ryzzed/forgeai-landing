Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a custom closet company book more consultations?

A chatbot qualifies closet type (walk-in, reach-in, garage, pantry), collects rough dimensions, and identifies lifestyle priorities — shoe collection, double-hang shirts, jewelry — so your designer arrives with a pre-scoped project. Homeowners browsing late at night can book a free design consultation instantly without waiting for business hours.

What should a custom closet chatbot ask to pre-qualify leads?

Ask what space they want to organize (master walk-in, reach-in, pantry, garage), approximate dimensions, what is currently not working (insufficient hanging, no shoe storage, poor lighting), and their rough budget range. These answers let your designer prepare mood-board concepts before the in-home consultation.

Can the chatbot upsell from a single closet to whole-home organization?

Yes. After booking the master closet consultation, the chatbot asks about secondary closets, pantry, mudroom, and garage — identifying multi-room opportunities worth $8,000–$20,000 that would otherwise require a separate sales touchpoint.
