Common questions

How do I configure the chatbot to stay compliant?

In your system prompt, explicitly instruct the bot on what it can and cannot discuss. For example: "Do not give specific investment recommendations or advice. For any question about what someone should invest in, direct them to schedule a discovery call." The bot follows your instructions precisely. Most advisors configure it to discuss firm services, process, specialties, and fees — and route all specific advice questions to a consultation.

Can the bot integrate with my scheduling tool?

ChatFlow captures leads and their contact details through the chat conversation. For calendar booking, the bot can share your Calendly, Acuity, or booking link and prompt the visitor to click it. This keeps the handoff clean without requiring direct integration.

I work with a specific niche — can I configure the bot for that?

Yes. If you focus on physicians, tech employees with equity comp, divorce financial planning, or any other niche — configure that context in your system prompt. The bot can ask qualifying questions relevant to your niche ("Are you currently employed at a tech company with unvested RSUs?") and filter for the clients you actually want to work with.
