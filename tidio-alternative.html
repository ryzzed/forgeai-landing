Switching from Tidio?

How long does it take to switch from Tidio to ChatFlow?

Under 10 minutes. Create a ChatFlow account, create a bot, paste your business description into the training field, test it, copy the embed script, remove the Tidio snippet, add the ChatFlow one. Done.

Does ChatFlow have a free plan to try before committing?

Yes — the free plan includes 1 bot and 1,000 conversations per month with no credit card required. You can run it in parallel with Tidio before switching.

Can ChatFlow replace Tidio's live chat for my team?

No — ChatFlow is AI-only by design. If your team actively responds to live chats through Tidio, ChatFlow isn't a direct swap. It's the right fit when you want AI automation without maintaining a live chat operation.

What platforms does ChatFlow support?

Any website that accepts a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Shopify, or custom HTML. No plugin or app store installation required.
