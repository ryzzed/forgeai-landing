What is the difference between solid and engineered hardwood flooring?
Solid hardwood is 3/4 inch thick real wood that can be sanded and refinished 5-7 times, but cannot be installed below grade or over concrete. Engineered hardwood has a real wood veneer over a plywood core that handles moisture and concrete subfloors better and can still be refinished 1-3 times.
