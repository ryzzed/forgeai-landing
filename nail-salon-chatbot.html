Common questions

Can the chatbot handle nail art inquiries and custom design requests?

Yes — include your nail art offerings and pricing in the system prompt. For custom design requests, configure the bot to collect the client's vision and direct them to share a reference photo when they book. It's a great way to set expectations before the appointment.

What if clients ask about nail care and maintenance between appointments?

Include maintenance tips in your system prompt — cuticle care, products to avoid, how long before a fill is needed. Clients who get good advice from your bot trust your expertise more and become loyal regulars.
