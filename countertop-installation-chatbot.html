Can the chatbot explain the differences between granite, quartz, and marble?

Yes. Granite: natural stone, unique patterns, heat resistant, needs annual sealing. Quartz: engineered, consistent color, non-porous (no sealing), slightly less heat resistant. Marble: most luxurious look, softer stone that etches and scratches more easily, best for bathrooms or low-traffic areas.
