Frequently asked questions

Does ChatFlow integrate with Zendesk for escalations?

Not currently. ChatFlow handles the conversation fully via AI and logs transcripts in its own dashboard. Native Zendesk ticket creation from ChatFlow is on the roadmap. If you need immediate Zendesk integration, that's a reason to stay with Zendesk Chat.

How does ChatFlow handle questions it can't answer?

The bot politely says it doesn't have that information and suggests the visitor contact you directly. You can include your phone number, email, or booking link in the training content so the bot provides that as a fallback.

Is ChatFlow suitable for e-commerce support?

For FAQ automation (shipping times, return policy, product info), yes. For order status lookups that require connecting to a database, no — ChatFlow works from static training content. Deep e-commerce integrations are a Zendesk strength.

What websites does ChatFlow work on?

Any site that accepts a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Shopify, or custom HTML. No plugin required for most platforms.
