Common questions

Can the chatbot give medical advice?

No — and that's important. Configure your bot with a guardrail: it answers questions about your practice and services but directs medical questions to call the office or consult a doctor. ChatFlow's topic guardrails keep the bot on-topic and prevent it from venturing into clinical guidance.

What if a patient has an emergency or acute pain?

Include an emergency redirect in your system prompt: "For severe acute pain or any emergency, please call the office directly at [number] or call 911." The bot handles this appropriately without needing to escalate complex cases itself.
