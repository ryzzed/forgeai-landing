The chatbot identifies the customer's primary concern first — then recommends the right solution at the right price, rather than overwhelming them with every option.

Configure the chatbot to triage by symptom: allergies and pet dander (high-MERV filter upgrade), musty smell (dehumidifier or mold remediation), dry air and static electricity (humidifier), bacteria and mold concerns (UV purifier), VOCs and cooking odors (activated carbon + UV).
