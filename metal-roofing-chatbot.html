Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a metal roofing company close more jobs?

A chatbot educates homeowners on standing seam vs exposed fastener metal roofing, explains the 50-year warranty and insurance discount benefits, and provides a realistic cost range upfront — converting price-shocked visitors into booked estimates. It captures homeowners actively comparing asphalt vs metal and positions your company as the expert before competitors respond.

What should a metal roofing chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask home size and roof pitch, what type of roof is currently on the home (asphalt age is a key driver), whether they are motivated by longevity, energy savings, or insurance discounts, and their timeline. Homeowners replacing a 25-year-old asphalt roof are the best candidates for the upgrade conversation.

Can the chatbot explain the insurance savings from metal roofing?

Yes. Many insurance carriers offer 20-30% premium discounts for Class 4 impact-resistant metal roofing. The chatbot explains this benefit and helps homeowners calculate the 10-year insurance savings, which often covers 30-50% of the price difference over asphalt — dramatically improving the ROI conversation.
