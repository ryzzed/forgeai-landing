What is the IRA tax credit for home battery storage?
The Inflation Reduction Act provides a 30% federal tax credit for home battery storage systems through 2032. A 13.5 kWh battery costing $12,000 installed qualifies for a $3,600 credit, reducing net cost to $8,400. Standalone battery systems became eligible for the ITC in 2023. ChatFlow surfaces this credit to make the purchase decision easier.
What size home battery system do most homes need?
Most homes use a single 10-13.5 kWh battery for essential load backup. Whole-home backup through multi-day outages requires 2-3 batteries. ChatFlow asks about outage history, critical loads, and backup scope to recommend the right system size before the consultation.
