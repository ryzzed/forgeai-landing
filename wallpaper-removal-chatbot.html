Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help wallpaper removal companies book more jobs?
A chatbot qualifies wallpaper type, layer count, and wall construction — then explains the drywall damage risk of improper DIY removal. Homeowners who understand that torn paper facing requires skim coating before painting are motivated to hire a professional rather than risk a $500 repair after a steamer rental attempt.
What determines the difficulty of wallpaper removal?
The three main factors are backing type (vinyl-coated releases cleanly, old paper-backed requires steam and chemicals), number of layers (each additional layer multiplies difficulty), and whether walls are skim-coated drywall vs plaster. ChatFlow asks these questions before the estimate visit so your crew arrives with the right equipment for the specific job.
Can wallpaper be removed without damaging drywall?
Professional removal uses controlled moisture, commercial remover solution, and proper soaking time to release adhesive without saturating drywall facing. Improper DIY removal with excessive water or wrong technique tears the paper facing and requires skim coating before painting — adding $300-$800 to the project. ChatFlow explains this risk to motivate professional hiring.
