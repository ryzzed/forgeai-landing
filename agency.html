Agency FAQ

Questions agencies ask

Can I use my own brand name on the chatbot?

Yes, with the Pro plan. The "Powered by ChatFlow" badge is removed, and the bot name, colors, and welcome message are fully customizable. You can present it as your own AI product or under the client's brand.

Can I create separate accounts for each client?

Yes. Each ChatFlow account is independent. You can create a Starter account per client (they each get their own login and bot), or use a Pro account where you manage up to 5 bots in one dashboard.

What do I tell clients when they ask what AI powers it?

ChatFlow runs on LLaMA 3.3 70B — a state-of-the-art open-source language model by Meta. You can say "powered by advanced open-source AI" or simply "AI-powered customer support." Most clients don't need the technical detail.

What if a client outgrows the message limit?

Upgrade their account from Starter ($39/mo, 500 messages) to Pro ($79/mo, unlimited). You can pass the cost increase through to the client or absorb it depending on your pricing model. The upgrade takes 30 seconds.

How do I handle training data updates for clients?

Two options: (1) Give clients their own login so they can update the bot themselves. (2) Keep credentials yourself and charge a monthly content-update retainer. Most agencies do a mix — initial setup by you, ongoing updates by the client.

What does the embed process look like for my team?

You paste one <script> tag with a data-bot-id attribute. Platform-specific steps (WordPress, Shopify, Squarespace, etc.) are documented at myflow.chat/embed.html. Your developers will find the WordPress plugin especially fast.
