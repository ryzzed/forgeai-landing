Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help an asphalt sealcoating company book more jobs?

A chatbot qualifies driveway or parking lot square footage, pavement condition, and last sealcoat date — providing an instant ballpark quote and booking appointments during the spring rush when homeowners are actively searching. It captures commercial property managers scheduling annual maintenance and residential customers prompted by winter damage.

What should an asphalt sealcoating chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask whether it is a residential driveway or commercial parking lot, approximate square footage, current pavement condition (cracks, potholes, surface oxidation), and when it was last sealed. Cracked pavement needs crack filling before sealcoating — flagging this upfront prevents callbacks and increases average ticket by $150-$400 on residential jobs.

Can the chatbot handle commercial parking lot sealcoating inquiries?

Yes. Commercial property managers requesting parking lot sealcoating often need after-hours or weekend scheduling to avoid business disruption. The chatbot collects square footage, identifies line striping needs (a common add-on worth $300-$1,500), and notes scheduling constraints — routing commercial leads to your estimator with full pre-qualification.
