Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help cabinet refinishing companies get more jobs?
A chatbot guides homeowners through the reface vs refinish vs replace decision 24/7 — a comparison that typically requires a lengthy phone call. By pre-qualifying cabinet material, door style, and condition, it lets your estimator arrive with accurate pricing and close faster.
What is the difference between cabinet refinishing and refacing?
Refinishing means painting or staining the existing cabinet boxes and doors. Refacing means keeping the box structure but replacing door and drawer fronts with new material and veneer. ChatFlow explains both options with price ranges so prospects understand your value before the estimate visit.
Can the chatbot help homeowners decide whether to refinish or replace?
Yes. ChatFlow asks about cabinet box condition, layout satisfaction, and budget to triage: refinishing fits solid-wood cabinets in good structural shape at 20–30% of replacement cost. If boxes are damaged or the layout is wrong, replacement may make more sense — and your team can present that honestly on the estimate visit.
