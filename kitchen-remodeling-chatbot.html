How does the chatbot handle customers who have a small budget for a big project?

You can instruct the chatbot to handle budget mismatches gracefully — if someone wants a full gut renovation but mentions a $12,000 budget, the chatbot explains what's realistic in that range (cabinet refacing, new hardware, countertop replacement, paint) and still captures the lead. Yes. You include your typical process in the system prompt: design consultation, cabinet lead time (4–10 weeks for semi-custom, 10–16 weeks for custom), permit timeline if applicable, construction phase duration (2–6 weeks depending on scope).
