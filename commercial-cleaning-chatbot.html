Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a commercial cleaning company win more contracts?

A chatbot qualifies building type, square footage, cleaning frequency, and scheduling constraints — so your operations manager arrives with a pre-scoped walk-through proposal. What should a commercial cleaning chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask facility type and size, current cleaning frequency, whether they have an existing service provider they are replacing, scheduling constraints, and any specialized requirements. Medical and food service facilities command 40-60% premium pricing — identifying them upfront ensures accurate proposals.

Can the chatbot identify when a facility is switching from an existing vendor?

Yes. Asking whether they currently have a cleaning service and what prompted the search is one of the most valuable qualification questions. Facilities switching vendors are ready to sign immediately — they have a deadline. The chatbot captures their transition window and schedules a walk-through timed to it.
