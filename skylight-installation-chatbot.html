Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help skylight installation companies get more jobs?
A chatbot qualifies the room, roof pitch, existing leak history, and fixed vs venting skylight preference — then books a free roof consultation. Homeowners with a dark kitchen or bathroom respond to natural light education, while leaking skylight replacement leads have the highest urgency and close fastest when contacted quickly.
What is the difference between a fixed skylight and a venting skylight?
Fixed skylights provide natural light only and have no moving parts — the most affordable option and best for rooms where light is the primary goal. Venting skylights open manually or with a solar-powered motor to release heat and humidity, ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and attic conversions. Tubular daylighting devices are the lowest-cost option for small dark spaces.
Can a chatbot handle leaking skylight replacement leads?
Yes. ChatFlow identifies leak urgency — staining, active drip, or suspected condensation — and determines whether the issue is flashing failure or glazing seal failure. Replacement leads are faster-closing than new installation leads and often combine a new skylight with updated flashing and interior trim repair.
