Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help vinyl plank flooring companies book more jobs?
A chatbot qualifies room count, approximate square footage, subfloor type, and waterproof requirements 24/7 — then books in-home measurement appointments. Homeowners comparing LVP vs hardwood vs laminate get instant education on durability, water resistance, and price, converting browsing into booked consultations.
What information does the chatbot collect for vinyl plank flooring quotes?
The chatbot captures which rooms need flooring, approximate square footage, whether any areas are wet zones (kitchen, bathroom, laundry), current subfloor material, and desired plank thickness and style. This pre-qualification lets installers provide accurate ballpark pricing and arrive at the measurement visit with the right product samples.
Can the chatbot explain the difference between LVP, laminate, and hardwood?
Yes. ChatFlow explains that luxury vinyl plank (LVP) is 100% waterproof and ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and basements; laminate looks similar but is not waterproof; and hardwood adds resale value but requires humidity control. This education helps prospects choose the right product and reduces returns and callbacks.
