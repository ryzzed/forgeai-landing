Frequently asked questions

Does it integrate with Mindbody or Glofox?

ChatFlow can link visitors to your booking system with a direct URL in its responses. Native Mindbody/Glofox API integration is on our roadmap for Q3 2026.

Can it handle class cancellation notifications?

The current version answers questions — it doesn't push notifications. For class cancellations, you'd use your booking software's notification feature. ChatFlow handles the inbound questions side.

What if someone asks a question the bot doesn't know?

You can train the bot to say "For detailed questions, please call us at [number] or visit the front desk." The bot stays on topic and directs complex questions to your staff.

Can I use it for multiple locations?

The Pro plan ($79/month) includes up to 5 bots — create one per location, each trained on location-specific information.

How do I update pricing or schedule info?

Log into your ChatFlow dashboard, edit the bot's training information, and save. Updates go live immediately — no developer needed.
