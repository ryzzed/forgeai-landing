Common questions

Will the chatbot book appointments automatically?

ChatFlow's AI collects appointment interest and can link visitors to your booking platform (Vagaro, Booksy, Square, etc.). For direct calendar booking integration, configure the bot to collect name, service, and preferred time, then forward to your booking system. Full native calendar sync is on the roadmap.

What if a client asks something I haven't covered?

The AI will do its best based on your system prompt and then direct the client to call or email for anything it can't answer. You can always update the system prompt to add more information as you see what visitors ask.

How much does ChatFlow cost for a hair salon?

Free plan: 1,000 conversations/month (enough for most salons starting out). Starter: $39/month for 5,000 conversations with no ChatFlow branding. Pro: $79/month for unlimited conversations and up to 5 bots if you run multiple locations.

Does it work on Vagaro, Booksy, or Square booking pages?

ChatFlow embeds on any website where you control the HTML — your main salon site, a WordPress site, etc. It doesn't embed inside Vagaro or Booksy booking pages themselves, but it works great on the salon site that links to those booking tools.
