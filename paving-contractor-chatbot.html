Common questions

How much does a new driveway cost?

Asphalt runs $3-7/sq ft installed; a two-car driveway (600-800 sq ft) costs $1,800-5,600. Concrete runs $6-12/sq ft, or $3,600-9,600 for the same size. Pavers run $15-30/sq ft. The chatbot collects driveway dimensions and material preference to give a relevant range before any site visit.

How does the chatbot triage repair vs replace?

Configure the chatbot to ask about crack type (surface cracks vs alligator cracking indicating base failure), pothole count, and driveway age. Alligator cracking means the base is failing and resurfacing will not hold long-term. Surface cracking with a sound base can be filled and sealed for 5-7 more years. Honest guidance converts customers into loyal long-term clients.

What commercial add-ons can the chatbot mention?

Configure the chatbot to mention line striping ($500-1,500), ADA markings and curb cuts (legally required), speed bumps ($200-500 each), drainage improvements, and annual sealcoating programs. Commercial customers who see you understand compliance and maintenance needs are more likely to choose you over a contractor who just quoted the pavement itself.
