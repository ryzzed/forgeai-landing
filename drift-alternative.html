Frequently asked questions

Is ChatFlow really a Drift alternative for small businesses?

For customer support automation, yes. Drift is enterprise B2B sales infrastructure. ChatFlow is an AI chatbot that answers customer questions on your website. Different tools, different problems — but if you're researching Drift to handle FAQ automation for a local or small business, ChatFlow is the right fit at a fraction of the price.

Does ChatFlow require any CRM integrations?

No. ChatFlow works standalone — you train it with a plain text description of your business and it handles conversations. No Salesforce, no HubSpot, no marketing automation stack required.

How quickly can I get ChatFlow live on my website?

Under 10 minutes from sign-up: create account, create bot, paste business description, copy script tag, paste it into your website's HTML. That's the entire setup.

What websites does ChatFlow support?

Any website that can include a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Shopify, or plain HTML. No plugin installation or app store required for most platforms.
