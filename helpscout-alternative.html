Common questions

Does ChatFlow create any email tickets?

No — ChatFlow is website chat only. No email inbox, no ticket creation, no routing. Conversations happen in the chat widget and are logged in the ChatFlow dashboard for you to review.

Can ChatFlow and Help Scout work together?

You can use both — ChatFlow on your website to handle FAQ automation, and Help Scout for email support. They're complementary tools. Many businesses use AI chat for first-line Q&A and a shared inbox for complex issues that need human handling.

What platforms is the ChatFlow widget compatible with?

WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Shopify, or any HTML site that accepts a script tag. No plugin required for most platforms.

How do I see what customers are asking?

The ChatFlow dashboard shows all conversations with full transcript history. You can review what visitors are asking and use that to improve your bot's training content over time.
