Questions insurance agents ask us

Can a chatbot give insurance quotes?

ChatFlow handles the pre-quote stage: explaining coverage types, capturing prospect information, and directing them to your quoting tool or scheduling a call. It doesn't issue actual policy quotes — that's your expertise and requires your licensing.

Which types of insurance agencies use ChatFlow?

Independent agents, captive agents (State Farm, Allstate, Farmers), and insurance brokers use ChatFlow for auto, home, life, health, and commercial lines. Any agency with a website can benefit from after-hours lead capture.

Can it handle existing client questions too?

Yes. You can train it to handle both prospects (coverage questions, quote requests) and existing clients (claims process, policy changes, renewal questions) — reducing inbound calls for routine inquiries.

Will it give coverage advice that creates E&O liability?

You control what the bot covers. Configure it to explain general coverage concepts and your agency's offerings — not to recommend specific coverage amounts or make binding representations. Direct specific coverage recommendations to a licensed conversation with your team.

What website platforms does it work on?

Any platform that accepts a script tag: WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, or custom HTML. Setup takes under 2 minutes and requires no developer or IT support.
