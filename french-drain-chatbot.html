Frequently Asked Questions

How does a chatbot help French drain companies book more jobs?
A chatbot identifies where standing water collects, how close it is to the foundation, and whether the homeowner has had interior water issues — then creates appropriate urgency about foundation risk. It books free drainage assessments before the homeowner patches the symptom and forgets about it until the basement floods.
What drainage problems can a French drain solve?
A French drain solves surface water pooling near foundations, soggy lawn areas that stay wet after rain, water seeping into basements or crawl spaces from outside, and downspout runoff that saturates one area of the yard. The chatbot identifies the specific problem and explains which solution fits the situation.
How does the chatbot qualify foundation risk from drainage issues?
The chatbot asks about standing water proximity to the foundation, interior moisture signs (efflorescence, musty smell, wall staining), and how long the drainage problem has existed. Water within 3 feet of the foundation that has persisted through multiple seasons is a high-priority lead that gets fast-tracked for assessment.
