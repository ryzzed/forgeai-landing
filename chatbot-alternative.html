Why small businesses choose ChatFlow

vs. Intercom

Intercom is for enterprise. You're not.

Intercom is a $74+/seat platform designed for large support teams. ChatFlow is designed for solo operators and small teams who need AI chat without an IT department or six-figure budget.

vs. Tidio

Tidio charges per conversation. ChatFlow doesn't.

Tidio's Lyro AI starts at $0.13 per conversation. At 500 conversations a month that's $65 just for AI replies — on top of the base subscription. ChatFlow is flat-rate.

vs. Drift

Drift requires a sales call. ChatFlow doesn't.

Drift starts at $2,500/month and requires a demo. ChatFlow has a free plan, a $39 paid plan, and you can be live in 2 minutes — no demos, no contracts, no salespeople.

vs. Crisp

Crisp is live chat. ChatFlow is AI-first.

Crisp is built around human agents. ChatFlow is built around AI that handles queries on its own — no need to staff a live chat queue or be online to respond.

vs. all of them

Works on any website in 2 minutes.

One script tag. No plugins required (though we have a WordPress plugin). Works on Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, Shopify, custom HTML — anything that lets you add a <script> tag.

vs. all of them

Topic guardrails keep the bot on-topic.

Set allowed topics and your bot stays focused on your business. Won't get sidetracked into opinions, politics, or off-brand conversations. None of the others offer this out of the box.
