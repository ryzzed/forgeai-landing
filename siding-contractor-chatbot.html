Common questions

Can the chatbot compare vinyl vs fiber cement vs wood siding?

Yes. You configure material comparisons in the system prompt. Vinyl: most affordable ($4–8/sq ft installed), low maintenance, wide color range, can fade over decades. Fiber cement (Hardie): mid-range ($8–14/sq ft installed), very durable, paintable, excellent fire resistance. Wood: premium ($12–20+/sq ft), natural look, requires regular painting or staining. The chatbot helps customers understand the tradeoffs before the estimate so the conversation is productive.

How does the chatbot quote a siding replacement job?

You configure price ranges by material and typical home size in the system prompt. The chatbot asks for home square footage or a description and gives a ballpark range. A 1,500 sq ft ranch with vinyl siding might be $9,000–15,000 installed; the same home in fiber cement runs $14,000–22,000. This sets budget expectations before the estimate visit and filters out mismatched budgets early.

Can the chatbot handle insurance claim siding jobs?
