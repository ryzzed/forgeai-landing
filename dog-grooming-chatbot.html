Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a dog grooming salon get more bookings?

A chatbot collects breed, size, coat condition, and last grooming date so your groomer can estimate time and quote accurately. It books new clients and recurring 6-week appointments after hours — filling your calendar without you answering DMs and texts all evening.

What should a dog grooming chatbot ask to qualify a new client?

Ask breed and approximate weight, coat type (double, single, curly, wire), last professional groom, and whether there are any behavioral notes (reactive, anxious, bite history). These answers let you estimate time accurately, set appropriate pricing, and flag dogs that need a meet-and-greet before the first full groom.

Can the chatbot set up recurring grooming appointments?

Yes. After completing a booking, the chatbot asks if the client would like to set up a recurring 4-week, 6-week, or 8-week appointment — locking in repeat revenue and reducing the chance they call another groomer. Recurring bookings typically represent 60-70% of a grooming salon revenue.
