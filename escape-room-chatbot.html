The questions groups ask before committing to a booking

Group size and capacity

Min and max players per room, private buyout options, split-group arrangements for large parties — the bot knows your room capacities and recommends the right configuration for each group.

Theme and difficulty matching

Describe your rooms, their themes, scare level, and difficulty rating so groups can pick the right fit. Nobody wants to bring grandma to a horror room — or seasoned veterans to a beginner room.

Age and accessibility FAQ

Minimum age requirements, parental supervision rules, wheelchair accessibility, claustrophobia concerns — answer the questions that determine whether a group can actually come, before they call.

Corporate and special event packages

Team building packages, birthday party add-ons, private event pricing — capture high-value group bookings that include extras like catering, photos, or debrief space.

Common questions

Can the chatbot handle real-time availability?

ChatFlow captures booking inquiries and can share your booking link directly in the chat. For real-time slot availability, the bot prompts visitors to check your booking page (Bookeo, FareHarbor, or your own system) or leave their contact info for a confirmation. This works well for most escape room operators — the chatbot qualifies and captures, the booking system handles the calendar.

What about groups with first-timers mixed with experienced players?

Configure your room descriptions to include the kind of detail that helps mixed groups choose: "Great for first-timers, satisfying for experienced players — the puzzles reward logical thinking more than prior escape room knowledge." The bot can then recommend rooms based on group composition questions like "Are most of your group experienced escape room players?"

Can I set it up for multiple rooms with different rules?

Yes — configure each room's capacity, difficulty, theme, age restrictions, and pricing in your system prompt. The bot can describe each room and match the visitor's group to the right option. For a venue with 4+ rooms, this saves significant back-and-forth that would otherwise require a phone call or lengthy email thread.
