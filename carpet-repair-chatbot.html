What carpet problems can be repaired rather than replaced?
Most carpet problems are repairable: rippling and buckling from humidity or improper installation (power stretching), burn marks and small stains (patch from remnant or closet piece), seam separations (reseaming with heat bond), and transition strip damage. ChatFlow identifies which repair category applies so customers understand repair is an option before defaulting to replacement.
