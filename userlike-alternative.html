Common questions

When should I use Userlike instead of ChatFlow?

Use Userlike if you have a customer support team that actively responds to chats and you need multi-channel support (WhatsApp, SMS, Facebook) plus the ability to route to specific agents. Userlike's strength is its hybrid model and multi-channel reach. ChatFlow is for businesses that want fully autonomous AI chatbot with no agent staffing requirements.

Does ChatFlow support WhatsApp like Userlike?

Currently, ChatFlow is focused on website chat via an embed script. WhatsApp and other channel integrations are on the roadmap. If WhatsApp Business messaging is a current requirement, Userlike's multi-channel capability serves that need. ChatFlow is the better fit when website chat automation is the primary goal.

How does ChatFlow pricing compare to Userlike?

Userlike Team is $90/month (4 agents). Their Business plan is around $290/month. ChatFlow Starter is $39/month flat — no agents, no per-seat costs. Pro is $79/month for unlimited conversations. For small businesses that want AI automation without a support team, ChatFlow is significantly cheaper with less operational complexity.
