Common questions

How does the chatbot quote hardwood floor refinishing?

You configure price ranges by square footage. Full sand and refinish: $3–6 per sq ft. Screen and recoat: $1.50–2.50 per sq ft. A 500 sq ft area runs $1,500–3,000 for full refinishing. The chatbot asks for square footage, current floor condition, and desired finish, then gives a range and books the free in-home estimate.

Can the chatbot explain refinish vs screen-and-recoat vs replacement?

Yes. Screen and recoat ($1.50–2.50/sqft): works only if no deep scratches and finish is still intact — adds new topcoat, cannot change stain color. Full sand and refinish ($3–6/sqft): removes all old finish, can change stain color, fixes most scratches. Replacement: needed only when boards are too thin to sand, severely cupped, or water-damaged. The chatbot asks qualifying questions and routes to the right service level.

How does the chatbot handle finish and stain questions?

Configure the chatbot to explain finish types (oil-based poly, water-based poly, hard wax oil) and stain options (natural, light, medium, dark ranges). Capturing stain direction during the chat conversation means your estimator arrives with the right samples — which shortens the consultation and increases close rate.
