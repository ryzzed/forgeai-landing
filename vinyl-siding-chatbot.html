Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a vinyl siding company win more estimates?

A chatbot qualifies damage type (storm, fading, buckling, rot at trim), collects home size and story count, and explains insulated vs standard siding options — so your estimator arrives with a scoped project and the homeowner already understands pricing tiers. It captures storm-damage leads immediately after weather events when homeowners are most motivated to act.

What should a vinyl siding chatbot ask to pre-qualify leads?

Ask what prompted the inquiry (storm damage, fading, buckling, selling the home), home size and number of stories, whether there is existing wood siding underneath that may have moisture damage, and their timeline. Storm-damage leads need immediate response; cosmetic replacement leads can be scheduled.

Can the chatbot capture insurance claim leads after hailstorms?

Yes. After a hail or wind event, the chatbot identifies insurance claim candidates by asking about dents, cracks, or missing panels. It explains that most homeowner policies cover siding replacement with only a deductible out of pocket, and offers to include an insurance documentation inspection — capturing the highest-value storm jobs before competitors knock on doors.
