Common questions

Can I configure it for both residential and commercial projects?

Yes — configure separate service descriptions for residential (home design, staging, renovation consulting) and commercial (office design, hospitality, retail) in your system prompt. The bot identifies which type of project the visitor is asking about and adjusts its questions and information accordingly. Different minimums, timelines, and processes for each can be configured separately.

Should I offer e-design services through the chatbot?

If you offer e-design (virtual, flat-fee design for DIYers), configure it as a distinct service tier with its own pricing and scope. E-design clients often have lower budgets and different needs than full-service clients — the bot can identify which tier fits and route each prospect appropriately. This is a good way to capture clients who aren't ready for full-service while keeping your consultation calendar for higher-value projects.

My process is very personal — will a chatbot feel impersonal to clients?

Configure the bot to reflect your voice and warmth. Interior design clients are choosing someone to work closely with on their home — the chatbot can express genuine enthusiasm for design, ask thoughtful questions about their style and vision, and create a sense of connection. Many designers find that a well-configured bot that asks good questions actually creates more warmth than a generic contact form.
