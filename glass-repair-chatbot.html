Frequently Asked Questions

How does an AI chatbot help a glass repair company get more jobs?

A chatbot triages chip size and location to determine repairability, identifies insurance claim candidates (most comprehensive policies cover chip repair with zero deductible), books mobile repair appointments, and captures homeowners with broken windows before they call multiple shops.

What should a windshield repair chatbot ask to qualify leads?

Ask chip or crack size, location (center of windshield, driver side, edge), how long it has been there, and whether they have comprehensive auto insurance. Chips smaller than a quarter away from the driver's line of sight are repairable — insurance identification is critical because most comprehensive policies cover repair at no cost to the customer.

Can the chatbot handle residential window repair alongside auto glass?

Yes. Homeowners with broken windows from storms, break-ins, or accidents are a high-urgency segment. The chatbot asks about window type and urgency — routing security-related breaks to emergency same-day service and standard IGU seal failures to scheduled replacement.
