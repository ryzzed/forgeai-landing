Loud bang this morning and now it won't open — I think a spring broke
A loud bang followed by the door not opening is almost always a broken torsion spring — the most common garage door repair. Do not try to manually open the door as this puts extreme stress on the opener and cables. Is the car currently inside the garage?
Yes — my car is trapped inside and I need to get to work
Trapped car — this is a priority same-day call. In the meantime, you can manually release the door using the red cord hanging from the opener track — pull it down to disengage the opener, then carefully lift the door by hand with both hands. The spring provides the counterbalance; without it the door will be very heavy.
