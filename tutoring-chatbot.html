Common questions

Can the chatbot handle SAT/ACT prep questions?

Yes — include test prep details in your system prompt: which tests you prep for, score improvement results, session structure, and pricing. The AI handles parent questions about test prep programs as naturally as any other subject.

What if a parent asks about a specific tutor's qualifications?

Include tutor bios and qualifications in your system prompt. If you have multiple tutors, describe their specialties and backgrounds. For detailed matching, configure the bot to collect student details and direct parents to schedule a consultation where you can match them with the best tutor.
