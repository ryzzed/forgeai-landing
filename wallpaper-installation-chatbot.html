Common questions

How does the chatbot quote wallpaper installation?

You configure price ranges by scope. Single accent wall (12–16 LF): $300–600 labor. Full room (12x12 bedroom): $700–1,400 labor. Removal before installation: $150–400 per room (more for multi-layer old paper). Materials separate: $2–8/sqft standard, $8–25/sqft premium. The chatbot asks about wall count, removal needs, and pattern preference, gives a labor range, and books the free estimate.

How does the chatbot handle wallpaper removal questions?

The chatbot asks about existing wallpaper age to set removal expectations. Pre-1990 paper: likely multi-layer, $300–600+ per room. Post-2000 strippable vinyl: $100–250 per room. Multi-layer or heavily textured walls may need skim coat after removal ($200–400). Pre-surfacing these costs prevents estimate-day sticker shock and builds trust before the visit.

How does the chatbot help customers choose between peel-and-stick and traditional wallpaper?

Peel-and-stick: renter-friendly, good for temporary accent walls, less durable. Traditional paste-hung: more durable, better seam alignment, far more designs, professional installation recommended. The chatbot identifies which is right based on the customer's space and tenure, routing each to the appropriate scope and pricing.
