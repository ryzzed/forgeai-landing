Questions attorneys ask us

Will it accidentally give legal advice?

You control exactly what topics the bot can answer. Set it to your practice areas and FAQ topics only. It will redirect specific case questions to "schedule a consultation" instead of analyzing facts or giving legal opinions.

Can it capture client contact details?

The bot can be trained to ask for name, phone, and email when a visitor expresses intent to hire. It then directs them to your intake form or booking link. Full CRM integration is on our roadmap.

Is this compliant with bar advertising rules?

ChatFlow is a website chatbot — analogous to a FAQ page or form. You control all content. We recommend reviewing your state bar's advertising guidelines with your ethics counsel before deployment, as rules vary by jurisdiction.

How do I update it when my info changes?

Log in to your ChatFlow dashboard and edit the bot's training information. Updates are instant — no developer needed.

Can I have different bots for different practice areas?

Yes — the Pro plan includes up to 5 bots. Create one for personal injury, one for family law, etc. Each can have its own training data and accent color.
